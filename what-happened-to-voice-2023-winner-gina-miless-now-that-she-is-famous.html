The Voice 2023 Winner Gina Miless Life Looks Drastically Different Now That She Is Famous

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1021 words  |  Patria Henriques

When looking at Blake Shelton's The Voice winners, an argument can be made that The Voice doesn't lead to massively successful careers. Gina Miles won season 23 of The Voice in May 2023. She has such a beautiful voice that fans feel hopeful about her future in the music industry.

Gina Miles is a relatable 18-year-old who tweets about her love for the reality TV genre. She became beloved after choosing songs like “New York State of Mind” by Billy Joel for The Voice and showing the world what she can do. Everyone knows her name now, and it's time for Miles to start her career. What has happened to Gina Miles's life after her amazing accomplishment on The Voice?

What Is Gina Miles Doing After Winning The Voice 2023?

According to Gina Miles's profile on NBC.com, Gina Miles lived in Sacramento in her aunt's house before she became a contestant on The Voice 2023. She began thinking about singing when she spent time with her DJ father who raised her.

Miles's story is inspiring. She really has a passion for the industry. It's amazing to think that the decision to go on The Voice has now totally shifted her life's path, and now she is experiencing fame that she never did before.

Since winning The Voice 2023, Gina Miles has continued to pursue her love of singing. Kcra.com reported that she performed at a graduation ceremony for firefighters. She told the publication, “It’s really exciting. I’m really happy to be home and see everyone and to do things like this with our community. It’s important and exciting."

Gina Miles praised Niall Horan for being her coach on The Voice in an interview with Parade.com. It definitely sounds like the two connected a lot and Miles was incredibly happy to have been matched with the former One Direction band member. Whenever Miles and Horan are interviewed about their time on The Voice, they have really sweet things to say about each other.

Miles also said in her interview with Parade.com that it would be great to have Horan be part of her debut album.

Miles said to Parade.com, “That would be the dream. And to make music that feels authentic and real to me. I have an EP that I put out before the show. It was very experimental. I was just learning about myself in the music industry. I hope to make songs now that are authentic to me now.”

In an interview with TV Line in May 2023, Gina Miles talked about life after The Voice and said that she was becoming more confident at literally raising her voice. She said that she talked much quieter when she was cast on The Voice. And then once she got more experience on the reality show, she knew that she had to speak up. She mentioned that she talks to the press a lot now that she has won The Voice 2023 and that has helped her with this skill.

When asked about the EP "Who Are You" that she made in 2022, Gina Miles told TV Line that she has been thinking about the type of music that she wants to put out in the future.

Miles said, "I put that EP out when I was a bit younger than I am now. I do love it, but I was young and experimenting. I just wanted to write and put things out, you know? At the time, I thought that was what I was going to do. Now, I feel like this is what I’m going to do."

Miles continued to TV Line, "I found the spot that I feel comfortable in and a place that I really like, so I think I’ll probably continue moving into this moodier side of myself. But I am a pop girl, and I love a good, upbeat record, you know?"

A full-length album could be in Gina Miles's future. She now has a bigger platform since she won The Voice, and she has 100,000 followers on Instagram. There is definitely interest in seeing her break out into the industry.

It's possible that in the next year or so, The Voice fans will hear more from Miles. Many The Voice winners continue to follow their music dreams and release at least one album after their time on the show is over. And now that fans know how great Gina Miles's voice is, it seems impossible for her not to record a memorable album.

What Did Gina Miles Say About Winning The Voice 2023?

Of course, everyone who competes on The Voice hopes that they will win. But it still must be a total shock when it actually happens.

Gina Miles's coach Niall Horan was proud and thrilled that she won. He explained in an interview with Entertainment Tonight that he really believed in her talent. Horan said, "I can't think of a bad performance she's put in. It's tough out there. It's not easy for any of us. But I'm going to try and make sure that Gina gets looked after, get her in the right rooms, let her write from her heart, and see what happens."

Miles sounded overwhelmed and surprised that she won, which fans can understand. She told Entertainment Tonight, "It was crazy, I just did not expect that. I'm just so very grateful to be here."

The singer also mentioned her father, which was sweet. She was totally real about what she wanted to do in the near future and said some things that any reality TV contestant can probably relate to. She explained, "I haven't slept in like, days, so I'm probably going to sleep and eat and call my dad."

The Voice 2023 winner Gina Miles always sounds happy about the way that things turned out. And she is so humble and kind that The Voice fans hope that her music dreams happen.

Made in Chelsea's Tabitha Willett expecting first child with Prince Harry's friend Fraser Carruthers

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  286 words  |  Patria Henriques

MADE in Chelsea's Tabitha Willett has revealed that she is expecting her first child with boyfriend Fraser Carruthers.

The reality star and the nightclub mogul, who counts Prince Harry as one of his friends, are due to welcome their baby girl in June.

Tabitha shared their happy news on Instagram by posting a picture of their baby scan.

She wrote next to the snap: "Baby girl Carruthers coming June 2019 so filled with love and can’t wait to meet her."

The couple met back in 2016, when she worked as a marketing director at his club Albert's.

It's through this venue that he became friends with Harry, as it's one of the Prince's favourite clubs.

Tabitha, who previously dated fellow MIC star Sam Prince, moved in with Fraser in November 2017, but they later broke up.

However they did't stay apart for long. and rekindled their romance last summer.

Speaking about her relationship, she told the Daily Mail: "I started working as a marketing director at Albert's Private Members Club in 2016 where Fraser and I met.

"It quickly became romantic but we kept very quiet as he was my boss. It was very on-off for a long time before we became official and moved in together in November 2017.

"But moving in was probably too hasty at the time as this led to us breaking up. I was broken-hearted dived head first into filming for Made in Chelsea."

Tabitha enjoyed a brief fling with 21-year-old Sam on MIC, but she says that she wouldn't even call what they had a "relationship".

Avril Lavigne Gets Cozy With New Boyfriend On Romantic Getaway

Avril Lavigne’s relationship history is a little complicated, but she seems to have finally found love again.

Not long ago, she spoke about what she looks for in a partner while refusing to answer whether or not she was in a relationship.

"The most important thing is finding someone that you're compatible with," Avril Lavigne said. "It all just sounds cliché. Someone that is trustworthy, because if you don't have that, you're just never going to feel grounded in the relationship, and you're going to be spinning. So that's just not healthy... a healthy relationship is ideal. I need someone who's solid, that I can lean on."

If her latest outing in Lake Como on an undeniable couple’s trip with a mystery man is any indication, she has finally found that.

The singer and her date were seen packing on the PDA at the beach in Italy. They shared a few passionate kisses and wrapped their arms around each other as they went on a boat ride with some friends.

This is Avril’s first public relationship ever since her infamous breakup with rapper Tyga a year ago, who she allegedly described as “a rebound and he helped her get away from her toxic relationship with Mod Sun.”

She has since had time to process what she went through and what she wants from a relationship, so it’s great to see her moving on.

Early Life

Taraji P. Henson, born on September 11th, 1970, in Washington, D.C., had a vibrant early life. Growing up with her two siblings, she formed a strong bond with her grandmother.

Initially, Henson pursued a different path, studying electrical engineering at North Carolina Agricultural and Technical State University. However, her passion for acting led her to switch to Howard University.

To support her education, Henson worked various jobs, including at the Pentagon and on a cruise ship. These experiences not only provided financial support but also shaped her determination and work ethic.

Throughout her early life, Henson’s dedication and ambition laid the groundwork for her future success in the entertainment industry.

Career

After graduating from Howard University, Taraji P. Henson embarked on a successful acting career that catapulted her to stardom. She made her breakthrough with an unforgettable performance in the film Baby Boy (2001), where she showcased her talent and versatility. This paved the way for her next prominent role in Hustle & Flow (2005), further establishing her reputation as a gifted actress.

However, it was Henson’s exceptional portrayal in The Curious Case of Benjamin Button (2008) that truly solidified her status as a force to be reckoned with. Her captivating performance garnered critical acclaim and earned her an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress, showcasing her immense talent and versatility.

Henson’s career has spanned a wide range of films and TV series, showcasing her ability to bring complex characters to life. One of her most notable roles came in the critically acclaimed show Empire, where she brilliantly portrayed the unforgettable Cookie Lyon. Her outstanding performance in the series led to her winning a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in 2016, cementing her status as a powerhouse in the industry.

Throughout her career, Henson has continually pushed boundaries and shattered expectations, proving time and time again why she is one of the most respected actresses in Hollywood. With her exceptional talent, captivating presence, and sheer dedication, Taraji P. Henson continues to leave an indelible mark on the world of entertainment.

Personal Life

Taraji P. Henson’s personal life is marked by both joy and tragedy. She has a son named Marcell, who was born in 1994.

Unfortunately, Marcell’s father, Henson’s high school sweetheart, was tragically murdered in 2003. This devastating loss shaped Henson’s journey as a single mother, and she has been open about how it has affected her and her son.

In addition to dealing with the loss of her son’s father, Henson has faced challenges when it comes to her son’s struggles with drug-related legal troubles. She has been candid about his difficulties and the impact they have had on their lives.

Despite these challenges, Henson has emerged as a strong advocate for mental health awareness, particularly within the African-American community. She has used her platform to emphasize the importance of seeking help and breaking the stigma surrounding mental health issues.

Bianca Censori became an international celebrity overnight when she married Kanye West in a non-legally binding ceremony in January. Since then, fans have been eager to get a glimpse into the controversial couple’s private life.

But according to a new report, the relationship hasn’t necessarily been a positive influencer in Bianca’s life, as it’s apparently made her more distant from loved ones than ever before.

Bianca Is Growing More Distance From Loved Ones As She Dates Kanye

Speaking to Us Weekly, a source claims that those close to Bianca are becoming concerned, as they can see a shift in the former Yeezy architect.

“Bianca’s friends are becoming concerned because their friendship with her hasn’t been the same in quite some time,” they explained. “Bianca has always been a strong, independent woman who has her own voice, but they haven’t seen that lately because they don’t speak or see each other as often as they had before she started seeing Kanye.”

Previously, a similar report claimed that Kanye has been trying to mould Bianca into his perfect woman, which includes having full control over how she dresses.

"Kanye loves to have a project and a protégé. He has previously influenced his exes' style, like Kim Kardashian and Julia Fox,” relationship expert Louella Alderson told Geo TV. “So Kanye might want someone he can easily mould into the person he envisions them to be.”

“With Bianca Censori not being famous, it might be easier for him to control and manipulate her image, something he clearly enjoys doing," the expert continued. “He also holds more influence over how the public perceives her and the attention she receives than he did with Kim and Julia,” Alderson added.

"He might be prioritising genuine interactions. With his recent struggles, Kanye may be looking for a break from the media-driven lifestyle,” the professional went on. “The attention he got from relationships with Kim Kardashian and Julia Fox might have been too overwhelming for him, so he's looking for a more understated, low-key partner who is more in tune with his current situation."

Being With Kanye May Get Bianca Into Legal Trouble

It’s not only Bianca’s relationships that may be at stake thank to her romance with Kanye, but also her professional reputation. The Australian native has been involved in multiple scandals since their unofficial January nuptials.

Recently, the couple caused a stir in Italy over a series of provocative outfits, some of which had Bianca’s private parts on full display. They also engaged in questionable behavior on a gondola in Venice, where photo showed the couple in a compromising position as well as Kanye’s bare butt exposed for onlookers.

There have been increasing demands for Bianca and Kanye to face a fine for public indecency, and it was even reported that a police investigation has been launched into their antics. The boat rental company even released a statement saying the famous duo were forbidden from ever using its services and that it didn’t support Kanye ad Bianca’s recent behavior.

For now, the pair haven’t directly commented on the controversy. They’re reportedly in the midst of making music and fashion deigns to support Kanye’s planned career comeback.

Abraham Ancer Age: How Old Is He?

Ancer was born on February 27, 1991, and will be 31 years old in 2022. He was born in McAllen, Texas, and reared in Reynosa, Mexico. He holds dual citizenship in the United States and Mexico. Sharyland High School in Mission, Texas was his alma mater.

He attended Odessa Collegiate and the University of Oklahoma for college golf. He has been a General Studies student. Ancer earned a first-team All-American and tied for second in the Junior College National Golf Championship during his one year at Odessa.

He had the most success at Oklahoma during his first year, winning twice and having the sixth-lowest scoring average in school history (72.03). He finished second in all-time scoring average throughout the course of his career.

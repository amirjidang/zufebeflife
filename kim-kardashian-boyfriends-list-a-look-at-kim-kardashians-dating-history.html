Kim Kardashian, known in real life as Kimberly Noel Kardashian is an American media personality, businesswoman, socialite and model. She gained media attention when a sex tape of her and her then-boyfriend Ray J titled “Kim Kardashian, Superstar” was on for sale in 2007.

Kim Kardashian was featured on reality shows like “The Simple Life” between the years 2003 and 2006. In October 2017, she and her family featured on a reality television series titled “Keep Up With The Kardashians.”

The love life of Kimberly Noel Kardashian has been something worth talking about and criticizing. She has married on three different occasions and has been in a relationship with a number of men. Check out the list of men who have been in a romantic relationship with Kim in the past.

In 2020, Kim was aged 19 and she tied the knot with Damon Thomas but the marriage lasted for three years. She also married NBA star Kris Humphries in 2011 but the marriage could not stand the test of time. It only lasted for 72 days. Kim Kardashian went on to marry Kanye West in 2014 but have separated in 2021.

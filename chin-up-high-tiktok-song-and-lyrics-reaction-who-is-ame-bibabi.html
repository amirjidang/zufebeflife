Ame Bibabi Wiki Facts And Age Explored

Ame Bibabi’s age is 33 years old as of 2021, per her official site.

She was born in 1988 and her birthday falls on April 12 every year.

Bibabi is Dutch but her song has become viral all over the hip-hop community.

To the surprise, the aspiring hip-hop artist is a medical degree graduate.

She graduated with an Orthomolecular medicine degree back in 2019.

On top of that, she previously worked as a teaching assistant at an elementary school. 

Bibabi was hopeful to be a successful health and fitness entrepreneur until she got bored.

As writing lyrics and finding melodies was her secret talent, she opted to start a music career and dropped “Chin Up Hight” in November this year.

Though its flaws, the repeated sync of the song has made the listeners hum this song for hours.

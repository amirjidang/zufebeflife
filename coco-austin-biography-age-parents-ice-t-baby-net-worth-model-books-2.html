Coco Austin Biography

Coco Austin (birth name Nicole Natalie Austin) also known as Nicole Natalie Marrow Austin and also coco T is an American actress, dancer, glamour model, fashion designer and web personality born on 17th March 1979 in Tarzana, Los Angeles, California, United States. She is the wife to rapper and actor Coco-T and is popular for her huge derriere which was proven to be 100% real in February 2012 on the show “The Doctors”.

Coco was brought up in Palos Verdes, Californi, during her teenage years together with her mother and sister they moved to Albuquerque, New Mexico where she grew as a tomboy.

Coco’s parents are Tina and Steve Austin and were both actors, they met on the set of Bonanza. She has a younger sister Kristy Williams and three half brothers.

Coco Austin Husband/ Ice T and Coco/

Coco Austin Model

At the age of six she began dancing (jazz, tap and ballet), her mother introduced her to the stage at an ealy age. She was involved in many productions at the Albuquerque Little Theatre in New Mexico.

At the age of 14 in 1994 she entered a model and talent search for Beverly Hills studios, in Los Angeles, California. She was among the five contestants who received a scholarship to attend the school.

At the age of 18 in 1998 she was attending school for massage therapy and was also a teacher for aerobic class. She began specializing in swimsuit, lingerie fitness and body modeling. She participated in numerous swimsuit competitions and modeled for calendars, catalogs and videos.

She entered and won her first fitness competition, Miss Ujena contest in Puerta Vallarta Mexico in 1998.

Coco put her modelling career on hold for six years, after she married Ice T, due to his busy schedule. During this time she ventured into website design and created her own website ‘Coco’s World’ which became successful receiving millions of hits a month making her an international internet sensation.

Coco shot the DVD for her 2007 Exotics Calendar in September 2006 which sold close to one million dollars. The calender help build coco’s swimsuit model profile and helped her secure the cover of Smooth Magazine making her the first Caucasioan women to be featured on the magazine’s cover, the magazine made most of its sales to date. She went on to be featured in over 50 national magazine covers and other publications.

She was featured in the celebrity centerfold issue of Playboy Magazine in March 2008.She also appeared on multiple television shows.

She walked New York City Mercedes Benz Fashion Week for the Sachika Twins in September 2010 for their Fall/Winter 2011 collection. She wore a dress that revealed her famous “bottom” which went viral being the most talked about dress of the season and brought major attention to the Sachika Twins collection.

Coco premiered her own off Broadway Burlesque/Dance show ‘Coco & The Vanity Vixens’ as its executive producer and headliner. The show received rave reviews and went on hiatus.

In 2009 Coco launched her clothing line, Licious, the line is designed for woman with curves. She was involved in everything fashion related for the line.

Ice Loves Coco

On 12th June 2011 a new reality tv show ‘Ice Loves Coco’ premiered on E!, the show chronicled the daily lives of Coco Austin and his husband Ice-T. The show run for three seasons before it was cancelled. On 4th February 2014 Ice-T announced on his podcast that the show was cancelled due to the couple working on a possible talk show.The talk show, which would be known as Ice & Coco, ran for a three week test run in August 2015 with no further pickup.

Coco’s Workout World

Coco launched her fitness app ‘Coco’s workout world , which is available on iTunes and can be found on all apple products, as a way to empower women. Her mission was was to bring out the inner goddess in women across the globe, helping them to feel good on the inside and outside.

Coco partnered with California Exotics, the world’s largest adult toy company, to start her new ‘Coco Licious’ pleasure products. She later partnered with Renee ERofe and launched box lingerie for women, Cocolicious Lingerie.

Coco Austin Books

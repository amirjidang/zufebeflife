Hey, Upper East Siders. It’s been seven long years since Gossip Girl stopped filming, and the withdrawal symptoms haven’t stopped yet. Though the world has plenty of teen drama TV series, none have enticed us quite as much as GG did. Even if we don’t buy GG’s real identity for a second.

The cast of the show have since gone on to do other things, most of them continuing their acting careers and branching out into other genres. Some of the cast have made headlines for other reasons too! Keep reading to find out every cast member of Gossip Girl’s net worth.

Blake Lively: $6 Million

Blake Lively played Serena van der Woodsen, arguably the most iconic of all the Gossip Girl characters. Serena is the perfect it-girl who messes up time and time again and yet always lands on her feet. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Blake Lively has a net worth of $16 million.

Since finishing up with Gossip Girl, Lively has undertaken a number of projects. Recently, she appeared in the thriller A Simple Favor alongside Anna Kendrick and Henry Golding, where she played two evil twins caught up in a web of lies.

Leighton Meester: $5 Million

The other main star of the Upper East Side is Blair Waldorf, the conniving and somewhat spoiled daughter of a NYC fashion designer. Blair’s friendship with Serena is tumultuous over the course of the show, but the two end up as BFFs. And we all know that the Blair relationship we’re really interested in is that between Blair and Chuck, affectionately known as Chair.

Leighton Meester, who played Blair, is worth around $5 million. These days she’s married to another former teen drama star: Adam Brody of The O.C. fame.

Ed Westwick: $4 Million

Perhaps the most complex character on the show, Chuck Bass starts out as the villain that you want to see disappear forever and ends up being the primary reason that viewers continued to tune in every week. Although Chuck does some terrible things, he redeems himself through his relationship with Blair and overcoming the wounds his father carved into him.

With a net worth of around $4 million, Ed Westwick has done some other projects since the end of Gossip Girl. Between 2017 and 2019, he appeared in the TV series White Gold as Vincent Swan.

Penn Badgley: $8 Million

Penn Badgley is one former GG star who has made headlines recently thanks to his work in Netflix’s You. In the creepy thriller, Badgley shows off his acting range that stretches from dorky and adorable to downright unsettling. According to Cheat Sheet, he is worth about $8 million.

Dan Humphrey is the outsider of the GG gang, so most people were shocked to discover the big reveal at the end of the series. We still don’t buy that Dan was Gossip Girl all along. It would have made more sense for it to have been Dorota!

Chace Crawford: $6 Million

Playing the super handsome and charming Nate Archibald was Chase Crawford, who is currently worth about $6 million. Nate’s storylines always tend to take a backseat to those of Blair, Chuck, and Serena, but how can you not love him? For a spoiled and privileged kid, he is so pure of heart!

Crawford has appeared in a few acting projects since finishing up with GG, including 2012’s What to Expect When You’re Expecting, where he acted opposite Anna Kendrick. In 2019, he appeared in The Boys TV series as The Deep.

Taylor Momsen: $4 Million

Little J a.k.a. Jenny Humphrey probably undergoes the biggest character transformation from the beginning of the series to the end. She gets caught up in the wrong crowd, turns her back on the people that matter, and eventually leaves town. Taylor Momsen doesn’t appear in the last few seasons of the show, save for a small cameo in the finale.

The former child star (you might remember her from The Grinch!) is worth about $4 million, according to Live Biography. The 26-year-old is also a rock star, fronting the group The Pretty Reckless.

Michelle Trachtenberg: $9 Million

No GG character irritated viewers quite as much as Georgina Sparks. Even though she has moments where she redeems herself, she’s still ultimately one of the show’s most annoying villains that you love to hate. Michelle Trachtenberg portrayed her brilliantly. She’s worth every cent of that $9 million!

Tratchenberg has a list of other impressive acting credits to her name, including Ice Princess, which she appeared in before doing GG, and 17 Again. Since GG, she has appeared in a few TV shows and films, including Sleepy Hollow and Guidance.

Kelly Rutherford: $1 Million

Kelly Rutherford portrayed the super sophisticated and privileged Lily Bass, the mother of Serena and Eric van der Woodsen. Lily is another character that undergoes quite a transformation on the show, starting as a self-absorbed mom who’s more concerned with what people think of her than the wellbeing of her kids. By the end, she becomes one of the most endearing characters.

Rutherford is worth around $1 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. In 2019, she appeared in Pretty Little Liars: The Perfectionists TV series, and also guest-starred in one episode of Jane the Virgin.

Matthew Settle: $4 Million

Rufus Humphrey is the stark opposite to the other parents of the Upper East Side. Even when he moves into Lily’s extravagant apartment, he keeps the down-to-earth coolness that makes him one of the most popular characters on the show. Matthew Settle, who does a great job of portraying Rufus, has a net worth of about $4 million.

Fans of GG who saw the 1998 slasher thriller I Still Know What You Did Last Summer might not realize that it was a much younger Settle playing the villain’s son, Will Benson.

Jessica Szohr: $4 Million

Some fans love Vanessa Abrams, while others hate her. Some love seeing her with Dan, others love seeing her with Nate, and others wish she’d just go back to Brooklyn. Jessica Szohr did an amazing job portraying Dan’s grounded best friend—one of the only characters on the entire show with any morals. Sources state that she’s worth approximately $4 million.

Between 2017 and 2018, Szohr appeared in Shameless as Nessa and has also starred in a range of other acting projects since completing her time on Gossip Girl.

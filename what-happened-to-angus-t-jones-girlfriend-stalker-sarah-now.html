Since TV shows and movies often include kid characters, there is a very long history of child stars rising to fame in Hollywood. For that reason, it can be very hard for child stars to stand out so much that people still care about them years after they grow up.

At the height of Angus T. Jones' career, he was one of the most successful child stars in the world. After all, during Jones' years starring in Two and a Half Men, he had loads of fans, and he won awards alongside his co-stars. On top of that, it also has to be noted that Jones amassed a truly impressive fortune.

While there is no doubt that there were a lot of aspects of being a star that Jones enjoyed, there were some downsides as well. After all, Jones has been forced to deal with the tabloids over the years and that can be extremely difficult for any star to cope with.

Some of the most notable tabloid stories about Jones that have surfaced have focused on his past comments about Two and a Half Men and what Miley Cyrus said about working with him. On top of that, a lot of people were fascinated when it seemed like Jones was dating a "stalker". With that in mind, it begs an obvious question, what happened to Stalker Sarah?

Did Angus T. Jones Actually Date A Stalker?

After Angus T. Jones rose to fame as a child, fans watched from afar as he seemed to go through his first relationship. Of course, Angus could have dated someone earlier without anyone knowing, but his first publicly reported relationship was with someone named Sarah.

Unfortunately for Angus and Sarah, many observers started to think the worst of his girlfriend, and they even went so far as to label her a stalker. Since virtually everyone agrees that it is really messed up to stalk someone, observers really should have had a major reason to call Sarah a stalker, right?

As it turns out, observers had a couple of fantastic reasons to call Angus' reported girlfriend at the time Stalker Sarah.

Before Angus and Sarah became a couple, she started to build a notable presence for herself on social media. In Sarah's case, the social media website she seemed to be most committed to was Flickr.

On Flickr, Sarah gave observers two major reasons to call her a stalker. First off, Instead of going by her real name, she gave herself the name Stalker Sarah on Flickr. Secondly, Sarah's Flickr account featured loads of photos of her alongside a slew of different celebrities.

Of course, if most people got a picture of themselves with a massive star, they would post it on social media for all to see. However, in Sarah's case, it seemed like she would post photos of herself alongside anyone that other people may recognize, no matter how small their fan following may be.

Despite the fact that there were reasons for people to call Sarah a stalker, the question remains, was that an accurate description of her? When it comes to that question, it seems like it depends on how people define what a stalker is.

When most people think of a stalker, they imagine someone who is dangerously obsessed with a celebrity. If that is how someone defines what a stalker is, Sarah never seemed to be that kind of person.

On the other hand, if people think a stalker is someone who spends a lot of time thinking about meeting celebrities, that is Sarah. Furthermore, Sarah has a history of going to great lengths to meet celebrities in the past.

Fortunately for any of the stars Sarah has met, however, she was very considerate from all accounts. Aside from a reported short-term feud with Demi Lovato, it seemed like Sarah got along with just about everyone in Hollywood. According to a Daily Mail article that referred to Sarah as Angus' girlfriend, a lot of stars even knew her by name including Justin Bieber.

What Happened To Angus T. Jones' Ex-Girlfriend, Stalker Sarah?

After Angus T. Jones and Stalker Sarah were linked in public, it didn't take long for it to seem like the two of them had gone their separate ways. Since that time, a lot of people who used to follow Sarah on social media seem to have no idea what she is up to now.

Similarly, in the years since Angus and Sarah broke up, lots of Two and a Half Men have lost track of him because he has opted to mostly live a private life. As it turns out, the reason Sarah seemed to disappear is she did something similar at one point as she took a step back from being a public figure.

After taking some time away, however, Sarah made her way back to social media, and she has gotten back to her old ways to a certain degree.

On Twitter, Sarah describes herself as a "Celebrity Photo and Video Journalist since 2008". It also is worth noting that Sarah has some sponsored posts on Twitter as well as she sells her followers on things like driving for Lyft.

On Flickr, Sarah has been posting photos of herself with stars again. Some recent examples of stars Sarah has posted photos with include Paris Jackson and Hailee Steinfeld.

However, just because Sarah has posted images of herself with stars doesn't mean that she hasn't grown. One example of the way that Sarah's image has changed is that it has now been reported that she is working on a book about her life.

On top of working on becoming an author, Sarah has started to regularly post content on TikTok. On her TikTok channel, Sarah posts a wide variety of videos including ones about the products she uses, ones where she discusses her past, and more.

Holly Marie Combs was so passionate about starring in The WB drama about three witch sisters that she altered the Charmed opening credits. No matter what else she does in her career, the actress always speaks fondly of her time as Piper Halliwell.

But since fans have always heard about fighting on the Charmed set, everyone wants to know if Holly Marie Combs and Shannen Doherty fought. People are also curious to hear if Rose McGowan and Holly Marie Combs have ever been able to get along given the intense work environment. Here's the truth about their working relationship...

What Was Holly Marie Combs's Relationship With Rose McGowan Like On Charmed?

The Charmed cast members have high net worths and they must have made good money playing their respective beloved characters. But even when actors are being paid well to star in a TV series, they can still struggle with their co-stars, of course.

While it can be tough when a new cast member comes onto a TV set where people have known each other for several years, there is no indication that Holly Marie Combs and Rose McGowan fought while they were filming Charmed.

The actresses haven't given interviews where they have insulted each other or said anything negative about the time that they worked together.

If Rose McGowan didn't enjoy working with Holly Marie Combs, it seems that she would be honest about it. She has been vocal about not being a fan of their other Charmed co-star Alyssa Milano. People reported in 2020 that the actresses got into a feud on Twitter.

McGowan tweeted about her co-star, "You threw a fit in front of the crew, yelling, 'They don’t pay me enough to do this s—! Appalling behavior on the daily. I cried every time we got renewed because you made that set toxic AF."

Combs was sad when Shannen Doherty stopped playing Prue on Charmed before season 4. According to Rostercon.com, several Charmed stars including McGowan, Doherty, and Combs appeared on a panel at the Paris Manga & Sci-Fi Show for Charmed's 25th anniversary. Combs said:

“I wasn’t happy with her departure and the writers thought that they could give me this sort of cathartic moment. So, there was my moment. It was pretty transparent.”

However, even though Combs was vocal about not being thrilled that Doherty was gone, she didn't take that out on McGowan or treat her any differently because of it. There is no indication that the two ever had trouble.

When Holly Marie Combs has talked about her time on Charmed, she hasn't mentioned having a hard time with Rose McGowan. Whenever someone shares that they had a negative experience, she sounds genuinely upset because she seems to have loved her days playing Piper Halliwell.

Krista Vernoff was interviewed by The Hollywood Reporter and shared that she decided to stop being a producer on Charmed because she thought that it was a sexist TV series. She said, "And they were throwing money at me, and the number keeps going up, and there's all this pressure, and all I can think is, 'I'm creating something that's now bad for the world, and I've had enough bad for the world in my life.'"

According to a People article from 2021, Holly Marie Combs tweeted that she didn't agree:

"I can attest 1000% Charmed was not bad for the world. The reasons and people are too long to list. Maybe it was bad for Krista's world at the time. End story. And the fact that we can still stand up for ourselves and the show and the people who loved it proves this. I never cared what producer or network exec wanted us more naked for their $. And still don't. We knew how to rally against it and found our own power. And still do. #Facts"

Are Charmed Stars Holly Marie Combs And Rose McGowan Friends Today?

Holly Marie Combs and Rose McGowan seem to be in a good place now. When the reboot of Charmed was announced, they both spoke publicly about not being happy about it. The fact that they were on the same page suggests that they have a good relationship. If they were fighting or mad at each other, it doesn't seem that they would have agreed with each other time and time again.

According to Digital Spy, Rose McGowan and Holly Marie Combs talked about the Charmed reboot for a few years.

Combs tweeted about the Charmed reboot in January 2018: She said:

"Here's the thing. Until you ask us to rewrite it like [showrunner] Brad Kern did... don't even think of capitalising on our hard work. Charmed belongs to the 4 of us, our vast amount of writers, crews and predominantly the fans. FYI you will not fool them by owning a title/stamp. So bye."

McGowan and Combs were both at '90s Con in March 2023, and they seemed to get along really well there. Shannen Doherty, Dorian Gregory, Brian Krause, and Drew Fuller were also there, according to Today.com.

On February 25th, 2023, McGowan shared a photo of herself and Combs that proves that they are friends. She wrote in the caption, "I really love my Charmed co-star Holly Marie Combs. This human means a lot to me. Blessed be forever!"

Even though neither star has said a lot about each other over the years, this Instagram post suggests that they have always liked each other a lot. Charmed fans can definitely rest assured that Rose McGowan and Holly Marie Combs are good friends now.

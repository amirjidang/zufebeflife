Sweet Facts About Rebel Wilson And Adam Devine's Friendship

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1445 words  |  Billy Koelling

Before 2012, most people didn’t know who Rebel Wilson and Adam Devine were. New on the scene, the two were working to make names for themselves as comedians and actors. Today, the pair are practically household names. While they have many talents and successes separately, collaboration after collaboration has shown the world that they do even better as a duo.

Even though there were many poor reviews for Pitch Perfect 3, Rebel Wilson’s talent still earned her acclaim. She was described as “flat out aca-larious” and credited for her portrayal of, “fat Amy Winehouse” (Rebel Wilson is a professional scene-stealer). While Devine has also built a fan base through his work on Workaholics and earned mainstream recognition for playing one of Haley’s flames on Modern Family, the best work out there is what he's done with Rebel Wilson.

The two support each other professionally and personally. Last fall, when Devine announced his engagement to Chloe Bridges, one of the first celebs to offer public congratulations was Wilson. Her comment on the Instagram engagement announcement was enthusiastic: “Adam, this is so exciting. I’m so happy for you guys.” Without any further ado, here are 15 aca-sweet facts about Rebel Wilson and Adam Devine’s friendship.

15 Their First Gig Together

Many people believe that Pitch Perfect was the first time that Rebel Wilson (who's been on a fitness kick lately) and Devine worked together, when in fact it was in 2011. In 2011, she was cast in an episode of the first season of Workaholics. It was her first US acting job and Wilson had to make out with Devine’s character while the pair were made-up in white and black Juggalo (Insane Clown Posse) makeup. It took 50 takes to get the makeout scene just right, so the two were very comfortable together by the time the episode wrapped.

14 Their 'Romantic' Friendship

The chemistry between Wilson and Devine is undeniable. A willingness to take on comedic and romantic roles is something they share, which keeps them working together. Devine says, “I get very misty. I like a good romance. I like to root for the underdog. So, I’m all for a good rom-com. It’s stuff you wish would happen to you. Why can’t I have a meet-cute? I only have meet-disgustings."

Related: 15 Surprising Celebrity Bromances You Never Knew Were A Thing

13 The 'New Emma Stone & Ryan Gosling'

While Devine and Wilson are not ‘typical’ romantic leads, their friend-next-door vibe is something a lot more relatable for the general public than the generic ‘Hollywood hot’ leads. Perhaps they are the next-gen Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling. Wilson joked about their infamous kiss scene in Pitch Perfect 2: "We were going for an MTV Award Best Kiss. It's all about the trophies!"

12 Their Comedic Spark

After their first time working together, there was a strong connection that many actors wait a lifetime for. Two people who love to work together, improvise together, and ham it up, like Devine and Wilson do, definitely belong together professionally. They've made some unexpected comedy, but they've probably also triggered some delays on TV show and movie sets, while trying out new things together.

Related: 21 Times Ryan Reynolds And Blake Lively Were The Funniest Couple On The Internet

11 Rebel's 'Work Husband'

Everyone loves having that favorite co-worker to hang out with during coffee breaks. For Wilson, Devine is this person. Devine said, "We actually have the same agent, and every time I talk to her, she's like, 'We need to get you and Rebel in a movie together where you're love interests...So, yeah. We're very comfortable with each other."

10 Romantic Leads Together

If fate had been a little different, we might not have seen Amy and Boomer come together in Pitch Perfect 2, mainly because Wilson’s character, Amy, was supposed to be with another love interest. Wilson said, “They changed it just a few weeks before we started filming and thank god they did because Adam and I are mates and I can’t imagine anyone else.”

9 Making Beautiful Music

Two people with similar talent sets and great chemistry can push each other to wonderful and exciting places, vocally, dramatically, and more. Devine said, “I’m hoping me and her just keep going down this path where once a year, we have a weird project together until we’re old people. It’s sort of a Richard Pryor and Gene Wilder situation where we just star in a 100 movies together."

8 Improvisational Genius

Rebel and Adam working together isn’t just about pushing past their typical limits - it’s also about watching...and helping others working with them to up their comedic games. Devine said, “Rebel and I have such a history with those “Pitch Perfect” movies, so we clicked back into it. And Priyanka — she’s such a pro, with her Bollywood background. It was really fun watching Liam because he hadn’t had much experience doing that. Watching Liam be goofy, since he’s the coolest guy on earth, was really awesome.”

7 Pushing Each Other Towards Personal Growth

Professional friendship isn’t just about pushing someone in their craft, it can also be about supporting and acknowledging their growth as a person. Rebel said, "I just think Adam is so cute and, just as a friend, I've loved watching him grow from when I first met him to now" and added, "(he’s) grown into such a sensible, caring man. Not that he wasn't caring, but he was a bit of a party boy when I first met him and I just love the man he's kind of grown into and so proud of all the work that he's doing."

6 Lining Up With Other Great Comedic Duos

A great comedic duo is hard to find, but when something just clicks, everyone from casting agents to audiences really feel it. Devine said, “I knew from the get-go Rebel was super funny. Then we did “Pitch Perfect.” Now this. It seems she’s Tina Fey to my Amy Poehler. Or the other way around.”

I personally am feeling more of a Catherine O’Hara/ Eugene Levy vibe from the two, but either way, I can’t wait to see what they do next.

5 The Thing About Bumper & Amy

Bumper and Amy are such larger-than-life characters that seeing them together is comedic gold. In terms of less-traditional couples in the movies, Rebel is quick to quip about Hollywood casting standards, saying: “I am the average size of the American and Australian woman, and yet plus-size actresses have less than one percent of all leading roles, I think, even though they are the majority of women.”

Hopefully, their work can open up doors for others, providing a wider range of realistic bodies that audiences can relate to.

4 Celebrating Their Successes

Devine and Wilson have done nothing but support each other, whether they're saying kind words about each other during interviews, recommending each other for parts, or connecting during their downtime. Hollywood would certainly be a kinder place if more coworkers were like this dynamic duo.

3 Getting Through Failures

Some projects fall flat. Unfortunately for Devine and Wilson, their movie, Isn’t It Romantic, was not well received by fans, scoring a mere 47 percent on Rotten Tomatoes.

Atlantic critic, Christopher Orr, wrote: “We now have an official subgenre in which a woman is initially presented as unattractive; is concussed into a fantasy in which she becomes impossibly desirable; and from the experience gleans important, affirmative lessons about believing in herself.” Hopefully, the pair have better luck on their next project.

2 Bad Ideas, Together

Adam and Rebel got together for a reality series called Bad Ideas, where Adam travels around connecting with celebrity friends. He and his famous friends try out stunts together. In the episode where Adam worked with Rebel, the pair donned cowboy and cowgirl outfits and rode on top of fake horses. They also worked on their lasso skills.

1 What's Next

While there’s nothing in the works that we know of right now, it probably won’t be long before these two 'partner up' again for a new and fun project. Devine said, "Me and Rebel love working with each other, and we have a great time on set. We have known each other for so long, and it is kind of crazy that we have done so many projects together, and I think the stars are aligned for us to get to continue to make movies together.”

Meet Alba Baptista: Marvel Fame Chris Evans' New Girlfriend

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  867 words  |  Tandra Barner

Chris Evans became a global phenomenon after he took the starring role in 2011’s Captain America: The First Avenger as Captain Steve Rogers. As he continued reprising the role in the Marvel Cinematic Universe for multiple projects. Along with taking on the famous superhero role, Evans has starred in numerous action and drama projects. With his good looks and lovable charm, the actor has garnered public attention for his personal life.

The actor has had several long-term relationships over the years. He dated Minka Kelly for over three years before they parted ways, and then Evans met Jenny Slate on the set of Gifted in 2016, where they hit it off. Slate and Evans dated for two years before ending on amicable terms. The actor wasn’t known to be in a relationship since 2018 but has a brewing romance with a young actress Alba Baptista. Let’s look at some details of Chris Evans’ girlfriend, Alba Baptista.

9 She Hails From Portugal

Alba Baptista hails from Portugal. She is Luso-Brazilian as her mother is from Portugal and her father is Brazilian. Her mother is a notable translator and traveled to Rio de Janeiro for work when she met Baptista’s father. The couple moved to Lisbon, Portugal, where Alba was born in 1997.

8 She Is An Actress

An actress known for her European projects, Alba Baptista, began working at 16 years of age, starring in arthouse projects and short films. Her first project was a Portuguese short film Miami, released in 2014. She was awarded Best Actress at the Festival Ibérico de Ciné for her performance. The actress went on to star in multiple films, including Patrick, The Child, and Magnetick Pathways.

7 She Got Her Breakthrough On Netflix

While Baptista was becoming a recognized face in Portugal for her project, she amassed a large audience when she bagged the lead role of Ava Silva in the Netflix drama Warrior Nun. She plays the role of a nun who wakes up with superpowers at the beginning of the series. Based on a comic book character, the show received critical acclaim for its storytelling and cast. The show will release its second season in 2022.

6 She Is Bilingual

Growing up with parents from different countries, Alba Baptista grew up in a bilingual household where she spoke two distinct Brazilian languages. Her mother, who worked as a translator, profoundly influenced her as the actress is also fluent in German, French, Spanish, and English. As her projects feature different European countries, Baptista uses her talent to give authenticity to her characters.

5 She Has Graced International Magazine Covers

Alba Baptista has had a steady career since the release of her award-winning performance in the short film. Her notable work has made her a sought-after celebrity for cover stories. She graced the cover of Vogue Portugal in October 2019, Journal Expresso in July 2020, GQ Portugal in September 2020, Revista Activa in September 2020, and Glamor Magazine in July 2022.

4 She Is A Humanitarian

Celebrities have always used their platform to make a difference, and Alba Baptista is no different. She has visited Cambodia on a humanitarian mission regarding education. In 2017, the actress visited Phnom Penh, the capital of Cambodia, where she taught young school students at an orphanage. In addition, she shared snaps of herself teaching children while playing a numbers game.

3 She Has Dated Justin Amorim

Before meeting and dating Chris Evans, Alba Baptista was courting director Justin Amorim. The Portuguese director cast the actress for his 2018 film Leviano where they first met. The duo often shared snaps from vacations and parties on their Instagram accounts. However, the couple reportedly broke up within two years as reports of Baptista and Chris Evans dating were circulated.

2 She Signed With An American Talent Agency In 2020

Before deciding to begin her work in Hollywood, Alba Baptista found the right representation to secure great roles. She signed onto United Talent Agency, based in Beverly Hills, California. Celebrities like Timothée Chalamet, Chris Pratt, Phoebe Waller-Bridge, Cynthia Erivo, and 21 Savage are also under the contract of the same talent administration.

1 She Has Been Reportedly Dating Chris Evans For A Year

Many sources claimed Chris Evans and Alba Baptista had been dating for over a year, but it wasn’t until his comment under one of Baptista’s Instagram posts that fans began getting a hint. Just as the rumors picked up the pace, the couple was spotted in New York’s Central Park in November 2022. The video showed them holding hands while walking alongside security with their masks on.

While Chris Evans’ dog Dodger remains his best friend and first love as he swoons the internet, fans have also gained curiosity for the budding actress that Evans has begun dating. Alba Baptista has shown her talent in European projects, but with Warrior Nun, she also established herself in Hollywood. The actress starred in Mrs. Harris Goes to Paris, which was released in October 2022.

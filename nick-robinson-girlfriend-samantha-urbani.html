After watching Nick Robinson play the main character in the beloved movie Love, Simon, fans weren't pleased to learn of his A Teacher role. The show is controversial as it tells the story of a student/teacher relationship, and Robinson played high schooler Eric who had an affair with his teacher Claire, played by Kate Mara.

Everyone always wants to know more about famous couples, whether they have been together for over a decade or they just started seeing each other. Nick Robinson has been dating Samantha Urbani for just over two years. Let's take a look at what we know about the actor's girlfriend.

A Musician

Some famous couples stay private and the same can be said of Nick Robinson and Samantha Urbani. While she has posted some photos of him on her Instagram account, there don't seem to be any couple selfies on his social media.

Nick Robinson and Samantha Urbani began their romance in April 2019, according to Your Tango.

Urbani is a singer who was in the band Friends and has also released music on her own.

Urbani's solo EP is called Policies of Power and, according to Fader.com, she left Friends in 2015 and began living in L.A. In an interview with the publication, Urbani shared more about the experience of being part of a band and then going solo.

Urbani said, "I started this band [Friends] and it sort of accidentally became a buzz band and I had a lot of internal crisis about whether I wanted to do anything that was popular or visible because on some level it taints making things and it's organic and compulsive in some natural way. Even though I missed playing shows and being in a community, I got back to a pretty zen place of writing song and not being visible."

In an interview with Paper Magazine, Urbani talked about how when Friends started in 2010, she didn't have an Instagram account, and so the world is definitely different now. She explained her POV and said, "I think it's easy for the actual music to be overlooked in favor of an artist's aesthetic."

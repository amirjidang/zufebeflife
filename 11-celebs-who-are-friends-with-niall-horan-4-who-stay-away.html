11 Celebs Who Are Friends With Niall Horan (4 Who Stay Away)

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1131 words  |  Martina Birk

Niall Horan, former One Direction member, has made a name for himself as a solo artist in Hollywood. He is getting ready to release his second solo studio album, "Heartbreak Weather," on March 13th. This record will include the singles, "Nice to Meet Ya," "Put A Little Love On Me," and "No Judgment."

He is friends with many famous people and talks about them a lot on his social media pages. He met some of them through friends. A few, he met on his own. Horan is pretty friendly to everyone, but there are some people that he just can't get through life without.

Horan is not someone who has a lot of enemies - he isn't one to cause drama, but there are some people he doesn't talk to. Mostly because they are exes or because they started feuds with him.

Read on to find out who Niall Horan's friends are and who he doesn't talk to.

15 Friends - Lewis Capaldi

This is a friendship that everyone wishes they had. These two seem more like brothers than they do friends. Lewis and Niall are always going to pubs together and hanging out. The duo is joining forces for the North American leg of Horan's 'Nice to Meet Ya' World Tour. Surprisingly, they met through social media.

14 Friends - Shawn Mendes

Horan and Mendes met backstage at an award show and have been best friends ever since. Many fans want them to collaborate, and they said they will eventually, but everyone is still waiting. Horan really loves Mendes' music and he has said that he sees him as a little brother. They constantly bounce ideas off each other.

13 Friends-Harry Styles

Since their 1D days, the ex-bandmates have always had each other's backs. Styles released his second solo album, "Fine Line," in December and Horan highly praised it. The pair is still up to their usual shenanigans and hang out occasionally. Styles and Horan support each other in their careers and personal lives.

12 Friends-Selena Gomez

After seeing photos of the two out together in the past, fans thought they may have dated at one point. However, that is not true and they are just good friends. The "Lose You to Love Me" singer supported Horan when his single, "Nice to Meet Ya", came out. She posted on Instagram about it. They have the same friends group and hang out a lot, but they've denied all romance rumors.

11 Friends - Louis Tomlinson

Tomlinson and Horan were close friends while they were in One Direction and they are still close friends. When Tomlinson released his debut solo album, "Walls," earlier this year. Horan told Twitter to 'get it climbing up the charts.' The pair also hung out backstage at Premios Telehit in Mexico and performed "Little Black Dress" together.

10 Friends - Fletcher

Along with Capaldi, this pair will also hit the road on Horan's tour. Recently, Fletcher and Horan recorded a cover of Taylor Swift's, "Lover", and it will give you chills. It even got Swift's approval. Hopefully, the singers will record more songs together because this is what the world needs.

9 Friends - Liam Payne

Although they don't talk often, the former bandmates are still friends. They did meet up together backstage at the ZPL Birthday Bash in 2017. They are both busy. Payne just released his debut solo album and is raising his son, Bear. Horan is gearing up for his next album and tour.

8 Friends - Taylor Swift

Horan is a huge fan of Swift's music and he lets everyone know it. He has said many times that his favorite Swift song is, "All Too Well," so he's a BIG fan.  They performed "Slow Hands" together when Swift brought Horan out on stage for a show during her Reputation stadium tour. Hopefully, a collaboration from them will drop at some point.

7 Friends - Julia Michaels

After meeting at the iHeartRadio MuchMusic Video Awards, the friends collaborated on the song, "What A Time." Michaels was the opening act during the European leg of Horan's Flicker World Tour, so they have spent a lot of time together. They had to get out of an escape room together on The Late Late Show With James Corden. 

6 Friends - James Corden

Like the other One Direction boys, Horan is close to the talk show host. To promote his album, Horan, like many in the past, will spend a week's residency on The Late Late Show, where he will do Carpool Karaoke and skits, and give a live performance. Corden has a special place in his heart for the 1D boys.

5 Friends - Rory McIlroy

There are fans of golf and then there's Niall Horan. He posts at least one story on Instagram when he's playing golf. Horan caddied for McIlroy, a professional golfer, at the Masters Par 3 Contest - they have become great friends since then. Now, if Horan joined McIlroy for a game, we might tune into golf.

4 Stays Away - Zayn Malik

After Malik left the band, Horan and the other 1D members did not really talk to him and they still don't. Although he has no bad blood with Malik, Horan just doesn't keep in touch with him. He is "doing his own thing", Horan has said of Zayn. Horan fears that if there is a One Direction reunion, Malik will not join.

3 Stays Away - Hailee Steinfeld

Steinfeld and Horan dated for a few months and then broke up. They kept their relationship and breakup pretty private. However, Steinfeld released a song called "Wrong Direction," which is thought to be about Horan. Fans are speculating that a lot of songs on his upcoming album will be about Steinfeld.

2 Stays Away - Ed Sheeran

Apparently Ed Sheeran and Ellie Goulding were a 'thing' and then Horan stole Goulding away from Sheeran. Goulding claimed that she and Sheeran were not dating, but Sheeran said that friends don't hold hands. Sheeran supposedly penned his hit, "Don't", about the two. Sheeran has admitted to forgiving Horan.

1 Stays Away - Donald Trump

President Trump threatened to punish One Direction after they refused to take a picture with his lawyer's daughter. They were not allowed inside the garage of a Trump hotel because they refused the picture. Horan has spoken up about not being a big fan of Trump. Luckily, Horan is Irish and has a different leader running his own country.

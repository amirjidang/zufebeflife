Kourtney Kardashian, the eldest of the Kardashian siblings, has made a name for herself as a successful entrepreneur, reality TV star, and social media influencer. With a net worth estimated at $65 million, Kourtney has the means to indulge in her passion for luxury automobiles. Her impressive car collection reflects her refined taste and penchant for high-performance vehicles.

This article provides a closer look at Kourtney Kardashian's massive car collection worth almost $4 Million, exploring each vehicle's features, amenities, and performance.

Bentley Continental GT Convertible - $202,000

Kourtney's Bentley Continental GT Convertible is a symbol of elegance and sophistication. Powered by a 6-Liter Twin-Turbocharged W12 Engine, this convertible can reach 0 to 60 MPH in a mere 3.3 seconds and attain a top speed of 207 MPH, according to Car HP. The opulent interior features diamond-quilted leather seats, cutting-edge infotainment, and bespoke audio systems. The convertible roof lets Kourtney enjoy the California sunshine while cruising in style.

Aston Martin DB11 - $205,000

The Aston Martin DB11 in Kourtney's collection is a true masterpiece of automotive engineering. With a 5.2-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine that can produce 630 horsepower, this grand tourer can sprint from 0-60 MPH in just 3.7 seconds. The DB11's sleek exterior, which boasts an Aeroblade, is matched by a luxurious interior, complete with hand-stitched leather upholstery, an 8-inch infotainment system, and a 360-degree camera system for added safety.

Mercedes-SLS AMG - $213,000

https://twitter.com/iseecars/status/515286181807140864

A modern classic, the Mercedes-SLS AMG in Kourtney's garage features gullwing doors as per The US Sun and a 6.2-liter V8 engine that generates 563 horsepower and 479 lb-ft torque. This iconic sports car can zoom from 60 MPH in a swift 3.8 seconds and attain a top speed of 196 MPH. The SLS AMG's interior is equally impressive, with Nappa leather seats, a 7-inch infotainment system, and a high-quality Bang & Olufsen sound system.

Lamborghini Urus - $220,000

https://twitter.com/Auto_Porn/status/1671618764402577410

Kourtney Kardashian's eye-catching $220,000 blue Lamborghini Urus is a high-performance SUV with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine capable of producing 641 horsepower. Kourtney flaunted her magnificent Urus in an episode of The Kardashians, which aired in May 2022, according to SCMP. The Urus' interior blends luxury and sportiness, featuring Alcantara and leather upholstery, a 12.3-inch infotainment system, and a customizable ambient lighting system.

Land Rover Range Rover SV Autobiography - $222,000

https://twitter.com/OtsileJK/status/1596092824575152129

The epitome of luxury and performance, Kourtney's Platinum Version Land Rover Range Rover SV Autobiography boasts a 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine, producing 557 horsepower. This powerful SUV accelerates from 0-60 MPH in just 5.1 seconds. The platinum version of this vehicle features a stunning exterior design with a unique front grille and exclusive 22-inch wheels. The cabin is adorned with premium leather upholstery, a state-of-the-art infotainment system, and a rear-seat entertainment package.

Mercedes-AMG G63 Wagon - $245,000

https://twitter.com/_CarMor/status/732312355539963909

The Mercedes-AMG G63 Wagon in Kourtney's collection is a rugged yet luxurious off-roader. Powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, this SUV can reach 60 MPH in a mere 4.5 seconds despite its enormous size and weight. Kourtney’s G63 boasts a black exterior, and the interior is adorned with Nappa leather upholstery, a 12.3-inch infotainment system, and a Burmester surround sound system, ensuring a comfortable and entertaining ride.

Ferrari 458 Italia - $245,000

https://twitter.com/RedlineDOorDIE/status/393101746244825088

Kourtney picked up a white Ferrari 458 Italia in Miami. With a 4.5-liter V8 engine producing 570 horsepower as per 21 Motoring, this supercar can sprint from 0-60 MPH in just 3.4 seconds, reaching a top speed of 202 MPH. The 458 Italia's cabin blends luxury and performance, featuring leather racing seats, a triple exhaust system, a see-through glass engine cover, and a high-quality audio system.

Ferrari F430 - $330,000

https://twitter.com/CuratorThrift/status/1672042214556585984

Another Italian masterpiece and the most expensive Ferrari in Kourtney's collection is the Ferrari F430. This exotic sports car boasts a charcoal gray paint job and is powered by a 4.3-liter V8 engine which can easily take the supercar to a top speed of 196 MPH. The F430's interior is luxurious and functional, with leather seats, a 6.5-inch infotainment system, and a race-inspired steering wheel.

Rolls-Royce Ghost - $337,850

https://twitter.com/motographer_JP/status/1672234225595203585

Kourtney's white Rolls Royce Ghost is the epitome of luxury and refinement. This stunning sedan is powered by a 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine, producing 563 horsepower. The Ghost's interior is a sanctuary of comfort, featuring wood veneer trims, hand-stitched leather upholstery, a 10.25-inch infotainment system, and a bespoke audio system. Kourtney has posed for several magazines alongside her extravagant Rolls-Royce ghost.

Rolls-Royce Ghost Black Badge - $400,000

https://twitter.com/duPontREGISTRY/status/1658502162173796353

The Rolls-Royce Ghost Black Badge in Kourtney's collection, bought in 2022, is a darker, more powerful version of the standard Ghost. With a 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine, this luxury sedan generates 603 horsepower and can reach 60 MPH in 4.6 seconds. The Black Badge's interior is adorned with exclusive materials, such as carbon fiber and fine leather, and a cutting-edge infotainment system. At the same time, the exterior boasts a white paint job and a Starlight Headliner.

Rolls-Royce Dawn - $410,000

https://twitter.com/AutoWin_com/status/1670672762367778816

Rounding out Kourtney's collection is the Rolls Royce Dawn, a luxurious convertible that offers an unparalleled open-air driving experience. The Dawn, priced at $410,000, is the most exclusive and expensive car in Kourtney’s collections and takes over 12 months to build. Powered by an all-powerful 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine, the Dawn can accelerate from 0-60 MPH in a mere 4.9 seconds. The convertible's interior is a haven of comfort and luxury, featuring handcrafted leather upholstery, a 10.25-inch infotainment system, and a bespoke audio system. The Dawn also features 20-inch alloy wheels and a power-folding soft top.

Kourtney Kardashian's car collection is a stunning luxury, performance, and style showcase. Each vehicle in her collection offers a unique driving experience, with top-of-the-line features and amenities that cater to her discerning taste. The Rolls-Royce Dawn stands out as the most expensive car in Kourtney Kardashian’s collection, with a price tag of $410,000.

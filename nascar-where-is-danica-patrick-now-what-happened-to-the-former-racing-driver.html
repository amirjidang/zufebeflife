NASCAR: Where Is Danica Patrick Now? What Happened To The Former Racing Driver?

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  421 words  |  Tandra Barner

Where is NASCAR Champion Danica Patrick now? Her net worth is in the millions in 2022. 

Patrick is an American former professional racing driver who made her NASCAR Cup Series debut in the 2012 Daytona 500. She is considered one of the most successful women in the history of the country’s open-wheel car racing.

Danica’s victory in the 2008 Indy Japan 300 is the only win by a female in an IndyCar Series race. Her best finish was 24th in 2015 and 2016, and her last race with NASCAR was the 2018 Daytona 500.

Where Is Danica Patrick Now?

Danica Patrick is quite busy with her business after retiring from her racing career. She launched her weekly podcast, Pretty Intense, in August 2019. The podcast releases new episodes every Thursday. 

Patrick owns a wine brand called Somnium. The vineyard is situated near the Howell Mountain AVA, in the area of St. Helena, California.

She founded and runs many businesses. Patrick also has an athleisure collection, Warrior by Danica Patrick. She has a luxury candle collection called a Voyant By Danica. In 2006, she published her autobiography “Danica: Crossing the Line.”

Danica made her acting debut in 2010 in an episode of CSI: NY. She hosted many TV specials on Spike TV, and she even got featured in the 2005 documentary Girl Racers. She voiced herself in an episode of The Simpsons. 

Patrick has appeared in several music videos with artists, such as Jay-Z, Maroon 5, Cardi B. She worked as a studio analyst for NBC’s broadcast of the Indy 500 in 2019 and 2020. 

Danica Patrick Net Worth Details Revealed

Danica Patrick’s net worth is around $80 million in 2022, reports Celebrity Net Worth. 

At the peak of her career, Patrick made about $10 million per year from salary, winnings, and endorsements. Her brand endorsements included Coca-Cola, Tissot, Chevrolet, and Nationwide Insurance. 

Danica was very popular for her long-running partnership with GoDaddy.

Who Is Danica Patrick Husband in 2022?

Danica Patrick does not have a husband in 2022. However, she is dating American businessman Carter Comstock. Her boyfriend is the cofounder of Freshly Meal Prep Service. 

Comstock graduated from the University of Arizona, where he studied business management at the Eller College of Management. He and Danica have investments in a wellness brand, Beam, reports US Magazine.

Patrick got married to Don Trail in 2005, and they divorced in 2013. She was in a relationship with NASCAR driver Ricky Stenhouse Jr from 2012 to 2017. She dated NFL quarterback Aaron Rodgers from 2018 to 2020.

What Is Bridget Regan's Net Worth?

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  444 words  |  Kelle Repass

For those who don't recognize the name Bridget Regan, a throwback to 2019's 'Jane the Virgin' is all the reminder they need. Bridget played Rose Solano, AKA Sin Rostro, and was terrific in the role.

That was far from her only acting gig, though. Regan started acting in TV and movie roles around 2006, after spending years on Broadway and other stages. She even had a brief role on 'Sex and the City' as a hostess.

But after a small role in films like 'John Wick' alongside Keanu Reeves and recurring roles in series like 'Legend of the Seeker,' Bridget earned quite the following.

And when it comes to where she ranks with the rest of the 'Jane the Virgin' cast's net worths, Bridget is pretty high up there. She may not be quite as well-known as Gina Rodriguez (or actors like Jaime Camil or Ivonne Coll, who both have a history on TV), but the actress does command quite the salary for her work.

Celeb Worth says that Bridget is worth about $2 million, based on an estimated salary of about $100k per year. However, because the actress is pretty tight-lipped about some aspects of her career (as well as her personal life), fans really can only guess at this point.

Married Biography also points out that Regan has been married to assistant director Eamon O'Sullivan, whom she met while working on 'Legend of the Seeker.' The pair married in 2010 and have two children together, but there's no detailed information on O'Sullivan's income, either.

From her activity on social media, though, it's clear that Regan realizes how lucky she is — net worth-wise and otherwise. Her IG profile includes a link to her Cameo profile, the proceeds of which go to Feeding America. Regan also signs photos of herself to sell off for charity.

She seems to split her time between NZ and wherever she's filming, but when she's at home, Regan lives a pretty modest life. Most of her photos are either of her and her family at home (or walking along country roads and looking at cows) or of her on set at work.

Bridget isn't the type of celeb to make headlines for her multi-million-dollar home or her glamorous lifestyle, which is part of the reason why fans love her. That said, the actress clearly has plenty more roles — and successes — ahead of her, so it wouldn't come as a surprise to any fan if her net worth quadrupled in the coming years.

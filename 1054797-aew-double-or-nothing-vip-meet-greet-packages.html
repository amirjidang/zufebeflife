All Elite Wrestling Double Or Nothing VIP Meet &amp; Greet Package Details Revealed

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  353 words  |  Billy Koelling

The promotion for All Elite Wrestling’s Double Or Nothing is now in full swing. Last night, the seating chart was released for the event, and earlier today, the pre-sale codes started being emailed to those who signed up for them.

Now, they have released full information on the VIP meet-and-greet packages that will be available to all who wish to go to the event. It is important to note that Matt Jackson said on Twitter that presale codes will not be necessary for the VIP packages:

$549 Super VIP Meet & Greet Experience in Rows 1-3 (Ringside)
  • One (1) ringside ticket within the first three rows
  • Pre-event walk down the aisle
  • Have your photo taken ringside by a professional photographer
  • Meet & greet plus a professional photo taken with All Elite Wrestlers
  • Access to our exclusive VIP lounge before the event, featuring:
    • Complimentary hors-d’oeuvres
    • Cash Bar with one (1) drink ticket
    • Comfortable lounge area to relax and eat
  • One (1) signed Double or Nothing event poster
  • One (1) commemorative VIP ticket and laminate
  • Early merchandise shopping
  • On-site event host
$339 VIP Experience in Rows 4 & 5 (Ringside)
  • One (1) ringside ticket within rows 4 or 5
  • Access to our exclusive VIP lounge before the event, featuring:
    • Complimentary hors-d’oeuvres
    • Cash Bar
    • Comfortable Lounge area to relax and eat
  • One (1) Double or Nothing event poster
  • One (1) Commemorative VIP ticket and laminate
  • Early merchandise shopping
  • On-site event host
$299 VIP Experience in Rows 6-10 (Ringside)
  • One (1) ringside ticket within rows 6-10; closest to the stage
  • Access to our exclusive VIP lounge before the event, featuring:
    • Complimentary hors-d’oeuvres
    • Cash Bar
    • Comfortable Lounge area to relax and eat
  • One (1) Double or Nothing event poster
  • One (1) Commemorative VIP ticket and laminate
  • Early merchandise shopping

$269 VIP Experience in Rows 4-12 (Corner of Stage)

  • Access to our exclusive VIP lounge before the event, featuring:
    • Complimentary hors-d’oeuvres

    • Comfortable Lounge area to relax and eat

Released on Hulu as a spin-off of the acclaimed TV show How I Met Your Mother, How I Met Your Father is a 2022 sitcom starring Hilary Duff, Christopher Lowell, Francia Raisa, Suraj Sharma, etc. The series was announced in 2021 with Isaac Aptaker and Elizabeth Berger as writers and Hulu as the network partner. The series is supposed to be a treat for all HIMYM fans. How I Met Your Father opened to a lot of flak from critics and audiences alike, but it was renewed for a second season which premiered in 2023.

Though the cast members of HIMYF are still not paid as much as the ones of HIMYM, they boast considerable net worth.

Tom Ainsley - $500,000

Tom Ainsley is a British actor who plays the role of a British Aristocrat, Charlie. He has already become a heartthrob among the audience for his stunning looks. Tom has a short career, and he has starred in a couple of TV shows like The Royals, Falling for Hamlet, and the French TV show Versailles. As per Married Biography, he has an estimated net worth of $500,000.

Chris Lowell - $700,000

Chris Lowell plays the role of an aspiring musician named Jesse on How I Met Your Father. Apart from this role, he has also starred in several TV shows like Life As We Know It, Grey’s Anatomy, Private Practice, Veronica Mars, etc. Apart from TV shows, he has also appeared in movies like Love and Honor, Complete Unknown, and The Help, which won him a Screen Actors Guild Award. Chris Lowell currently has a modest net worth of $700,000 as per Celebrity Net Worth.

Tien Tran - $2 Million

Tien Tran stars as Ellen, Jesse’s adoptive sister, in How I Met Your Father. Before her role in HIMYF, she appeared in a couple of guest roles in TV shows like Easy, Hot Date, Sherman's Showcase, and Space Force. Apart from being an actress, she is also a writer and has worked as a staff writer in the second season of Work in Progress. While much is not known about Trian’s net worth, she has a rumored net worth of an estimated $2 Million.

Francia Raisa - $3 Million

Francia Raisa is known for her roles in Bring It On All or Nothing, The Secret Life of the American Teenager, and Grown-ish, and she was seen most recently in How I Met Your Father playing the role of Valentina. During her High school senior year, Raisa landed the lead role in the 2006 TV movie Bring It On: All or Nothing playing the role of Leti. She has garnered a net worth of $3 Million to date owing to her frequent appearances in movies and TV shows.

Suraj Sharma - $5 Million

Suraj Sharma is an Indian actor who shot to global fame after starring in the titular role in 2012 with the Ang-lee-directed Academy Award-winning movie Life Of Pi. The film also won a BAFTA nomination for Sharma, and he went on to bag a role in Season 4 of the hit TV show Homeland. He has since starred in movies and TV shows like Million Dollar Arm and God Friended Me and currently stars as Sid in HIMYF. Over his short yet prominent career, Suraj has accumulated a net worth of $5 Million, as per Taddlr.

Josh Peck - $6 Million

Josh Peck is an actor and comedian who plays the recurring role of Drew in the spin-off series How I Met Your Father. He started his career as a child actor in the 90s and starred in The Amanda Show from 2000 to 2002. She rose to prominence after starring in Drake & Josh and lending his voice as Eddie for the Ice Age franchise. He is also a prolific Youtuber with more than $3 Million subscribers. Josh, and his successful movie and Youtube career, have amassed a $6 Million net worth to date.

Hilary Duff - $25 Million

Hilary Duff plays the titular role of Sophie in How I Met Your Father. She shot to fame during her teenage years and currently is a 4-time Teen-choice award-winning actress and singer known for her work in Lizzie McGuire, Agent Cody Banks, A Cinderella Story, etc. Hilary earned $15000 an episode during her stint in Lizzie McGuire and around $1 Million for the Lizzie McGuire Movie. She has now established herself as a credible actress with an impressive net worth of $25 Million, as reported by The Things.

Kim Cattrall - $40 Million

Kim Cattrall plays the role of the future Sophie in How I Met Your Father. She is the most experienced cast member in the show, with a career spanning decades. With her enormous filmography and work in a long list of TV shows, Cattrall has garnered a massive net worth of $40 Million, making her the richest cast member of HIMYF. She is primarily known for her work in Star Trek VI: The Undiscovered Country, The Ghost Writer, and Sex And The City, for which she received multiple Emmy nominations and a Golden Globe win.

The cast of How I Met Your Father is quite the fusion of veteran actors as well as new and budding actors. The net worth and popularity of the cast members might be less than the parent show How I Met Your Mother, but there is no dearth of talent or potential.

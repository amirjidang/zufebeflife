Cruise ships are large passenger vessels designed for leisure travel, offering passengers a vacation experience at sea. Building cruise ships is expensive because every cruise line desires the best for its passengers. The companies invest millions of dollars in the building of cruise ships. With various facilities and entertainment options, including live performances, these ships are like floating resorts that entertain guests as they travel.

Mariah Carey, Bonnie Tyler, Jennifer Lopez, and other outstanding artists have performed aboard cruise ships, entertaining passengers and making the voyage memorable. Please relax as we cruise through the world's top 8 most expensive cruise ships.

Quantum of The Seas - $1 billion

This luxurious ship, owned by Royal Caribbean International, was built at an estimated cost of $1.1 billion. Quantum of the Seas features innovative amenities like a skydiving simulator, a North Star observation capsule, and robotic bartenders. Quantum of the Seas offers various itineraries, including Caribbean, Asia, and Alaska destinations, depending on the time of year. It provides thrilling activities, entertainment options, and modern amenities for an unforgettable cruise experience.

Norwegian Epic - $1.2 Billion

Norwegian Epic is a cruise ship operated by Norwegian Cruise Line (NCL). It was built at an estimated cost of $1.2 billion at the STX shipyard in Saint-Nazaire, France, and started service in 2010. It is part of NCL's Breakaway Plus series of ships. Norwegian Epic has a length of approximately 1,081 feet (329 meters) and can accommodate up to 4,100 passengers at double occupancy. It has a total guest capacity of around 5,183, with all the berths occupied. Celebrities like Kid Rock have performed for passengers on Norwegian Cruise Line as part of his yearly Kid Rock's Chillin event.

MSC World Europa - $1.25 billion

MSC World Europa is a cruise ship built by MSC Cruises at a cost of around $1.25 billion and started service in 2022. Its maiden cruise was on 20th Dec 2022, with a 9-day trip from Qatar, where it served as a hotel ship during the 2022 FIFA World Cup. It consists of 2700 cabins and a passenger capacity of 5,400. MSC World Europa is a beautiful and world-class cruise ship under MSC Cruises.

The Wonder of The Seas - $1.35 Billion

The Wonder of the Seas is the fifth ship under the Royal Caribbean Class of cruise ships. The vessel started operation in 2022 and is the largest in the world by gross tonnage. It measures 362 m in length and has a gross tonnage of 236,857 across all its 18 decks. The ship has an accommodation capacity of 6,988 passengers and 2,300 crews. The Wonder of the Seas has a variety of dining venues, ranging from casual buffets to specialty restaurants serving different cuisines. It also offers Broadway-style shows, live music, nightclubs, and bars for evening entertainment.

Symphony of The Seas - $1.35 billion

Symphony of the Seas is a cruise ship operated by Royal Caribbean International. It is a part of Royal Caribbean's Oasis class of ships, known for its large size and innovative features. Symphony of the Seas has a length of approximately 1,188 feet (362 meters) and can accommodate up to 5,518 passengers at double occupancy. It has a total guest capacity of around 6,680, with all the berths occupied. Symphony of the Seas is outstanding in design, craftsmanship, and customer service.

Harmony Of the Seas - $1.35 Billion

Harmony of the Seas, another Royal Caribbean International ship, cost over $1.2 billion and started service in 2016. It is one of the largest cruise ships globally and offers various onboard amenities and entertainment options. Harmony of the Seas has a length of approximately 1,188 feet (362 meters) and can accommodate up to 5,497 passengers at double occupancy.

Oasis of The Seas - $1.4 Billion

Oasis of the Seas is a cruise ship operated by Royal Caribbean International. It is the first ship in Royal Caribbean's Oasis class launched in 2009. Oasis of the Seas revolutionized the cruise industry with its size and innovative features. The construction of Oasis of the Seas cost around $1.4 billion. It was the largest cruise ship in the world at the time of its launch and can accommodate over 5,400 passengers.

The allure of The Seas - $1.5 billion

The Allure of the Seas is the most expensive cruise ship, measuring over 1,180 feet long. It offers a range of activities, including zip-lining, surf simulators, and an open-air theater. The allure of the Seas provides a variety of entertainment choices, including Broadway-style plays, ice skating performances, live music, and themed parties. Taylor Swift performed at the Aqua Theater in 2011 in front of 700 people, making it one of the best concerts staged at Allure of the Seas.

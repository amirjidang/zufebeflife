GCW vs. New South Results (1/22): Blake Christian, Tony Deppen, Bussy, And More

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  229 words  |  Billy Koelling

On January 22, and New South Pro Wrestling joined forces to present the GCW vs. New South event.

The show was held at Singin’ River Brewing Company in Florence, Alabama, and it featured several inter-promotional matches. Blake Christian, Tony Deppen, and other stars competed on the event, which aired on FITE Plus.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • GCW World Champion Nick Gage hyped up the crowd before the first match.
  • Jordan Oliver def. Tyler Franks
  • Cabana Man Dan def. Tony Deppen
  • Sawyer Wreck def. Donnie Primetime
  • : Kenzie Paige (c) def. Billie Starks
  • : Team New South (Rolando Perez, Kevin Ryan, Steven Michaels, Chris Crunk, Brayden Toon & Marcus Dylan) def. Team GCW (Jimmy Lloyd, Gringo Loco, Cole Radrick, Yoya, Marcus Mathers & Arez)
  • Bussy (Allie Katch & Effy) def. The Carnies (Kerry Awful & Nick Iggy)
  • Blake Christian def. Hunter Drake
  • GCW World Tag Team Champions Los Macizos def. New South Tag Team Champions Infrared (Tyler Matrix & Logan James) in a non-title match.

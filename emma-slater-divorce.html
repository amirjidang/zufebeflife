The Dancer And DWTS Emma Slater Files Divorce From Husband Sasha Farber!

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  531 words  |  Tandra Barner

Emma Slater and Sasha Farber have decided to divorce. On February 21, the Dancing With the Stars pro filed for divorce in Los Angeles, according to court records.  The separation was due to irreconcilable differences, according to Slater.

Court records indicate that they separated on April 1, 2022, while a source claimed that they had split up in August 2022.

Slater, who wed Farber in 2018, requested the court to stop granting spousal support for her and Farber. Slater and Farber want to “amicably” handle any disagreements regarding property and assets in a contract, with Slater maintaining the right to make changes as needed.

The former couple reportedly went through an “uncontested divorce,” according to court documents. Neither celeb has addressed the split in the media.

Journey Of Emma Slater And Sasha Farber Together

The Dancing With the Stars couple first connected while filming Burn The Floor on Broadway, and two years later, in 2011, they made their relationship official. 

Yet after three years together, they decided to call it quits in 2014, choosing instead to be friends and collaborate frequently. Emma Slater revealed that she loves Sasha Farber dearly and that they were still living together when they had a brief breakup. 

The dancer went on to say that he was the best person and that if she hadn’t loved him so much for those three years, she wouldn’t have been so close to him.

She conveyed her appreciation for him and her contentment with their living situation. Both of them, according to her, understood things weren’t going as they should, so they developed and made that decision.

They were still in love, she said, and he was the only person she had in America. She wanted the best for him, so they made the decision to continue being there for one another.

The DWTS couple made up and decided to try again in love a year later, in 2015, and Slater then proposed. In an interview, Farber claimed that the marriage of Emma and Sasha, which took place in March 2018, was straight out of a fairy tale.

“It was the most amazing day. It was absolutely amazing. Nothing went wrong. It was brilliant. It was incredible. I kept thinking, ‘This isn’t normal. This isn’t normal. This should not be happening.’ It went so well and it was a party from the moment we got upstairs to the reception till really late.”

Early in 2022, curious followers observed the longtime partners weren’t being featured on each other’s social media profiles, which led to divorce rumors. 

The couple made headlines once more in March 2022 when they neglected to upload an Instagram anniversary picture. Slater did, however, profess her love for Farber on his birthday two months later.

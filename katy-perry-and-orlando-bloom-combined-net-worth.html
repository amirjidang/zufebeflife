Hollywood star Orlando Bloom and pop princess Katy Perry started dating back in 2016, and they've been one of the industry's most famous couples ever since. In 2019 the couple got engaged, and in 2020, their daughter Daisy Dove Bloom was born.

Today, we're taking a look at all the ways that Orlando and Katy are earning and spending their money. Orlando is estimated to have a net worth of $40 million, while Katy is estimated to have a net worth of $330 million. This means that the couple's combined net worth is $370 million.

If you're wondering how they got there and what they spend it on, keep on scrolling!

10 Earn: Orlando Bloom's Acting

Kicking the list off is the fact that a big chunk of their combined net worth comes from Orlando's acting roles. The English actor is best known for starring in franchises like The Lord of the Rings and Pirates of the Caribbean, as well as for his work on Troy, Kingdom of Heaven, and many more.

Since the early 2000s, Orlando Bloom has certainly established himself as a staple in Tinseltown.

One thing that Orlando spent quite a lot of money on is Katy's engagement ring. The famous duo got engaged on Valentine's Day 2019 — and depending on whether the gem on her ring is a ruby, pink diamond, or pink sapphire, expert valuations have fluctuated.

7 Spend: Orlando Bloom And Katy Perry's Home

Like most celebrity couples, Katy and Orlando have also invested in real estate together. In 2021, the pair paid $14.2 million for a massive luxurious estate boasting nearly nine acres of grounds.

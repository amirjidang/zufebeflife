Thanks to how active Chrissy Teigen has been on social media over the years, it often seems like fans know just about everything about her personal life. A perfect example of that is the fact that there was a lot of excitement because fans knew Teigen was expecting a baby in 2023.

Similarly, it was big news when Teigen revealed that she recently briefly believed she had an identical twin. While Teigen's belief she had a twin turned out to be wrong, there is no doubt that the supermodel has a sister named Tina Teigen.

Why Did Chrissy Teigen Think She Had An Identical Twin?

Throughout most of history, anyone who wanted to know where they came from could only ask their parents. Aside from the people whose entire family is from a single area, everyone else could only hope that their parents remembered their background accurately.

Over the last several years, all that has been changing because several ancestry companies have popped up. These days, all that anyone who wants to accurately know about their genetic background has to do is pay a fee and send a DNA sample to one of those companies.

On top of people being able to learn more about where their relatives came from, many ancestry companies can also inform customers about their family's medical history. Because of that latter service, Chrissy Teigen found herself in a shocking situation in June 2023.

On June 5th, 2023, Teigen uploaded a video on Instagram where she spoke about what she'd been through in the preceding 24 hours. As Teigen explained, she suddenly found herself questioning a lot about her past after getting results from an ancestry company.

At the start of Teigen's Instagram video, she explains that she has been on a journey of self-discovery in recent months. For her, that has meant that she has wanted to learn more about her own health and where her family is from.

After speaking to her therapist and doctor, Teigen decided to sign up for 23andMe's services. As Teigen explained in her video, she'd previously used 23andMe's service to find out about her ancestry but this time she paid to learn more about her health.

According to Teigen, she started "spiraling" after getting her 23andMe results which revealed she has an identical twin sister she knew nothing about. As Teigen described in her Instagram video, she called her dad to question him about the situation.

“I call my dad and I literally say, ‘Dad, hey, did you see me come out of Mom’s vagina and were there two of me?' He starts laughing and saying, ‘No, I was there. There were not two of you.’ I’m like, ‘Are you sure you were there?'”

Evidently, Teigen's father saying that she didn't have a twin sister wasn't enough confirmation for her. Perhaps Teigen thought that, unlike Lisa Marie Presley's ex Michael Lockwood who gets along with their twin daughters, her parents thought having twins would be too much. Either way, Teigen called her sister Tina Teigen to talk to her about her 23andMe results.

As Teigen explained in her Instagram video, she told her sister what was going on and then asked if she had a twin. Instead of Tina Teigen laughing or instantly saying no, Teigen says her sister reacted to that question by letting out a big gasp.

When Teigen heard her sister gasp, she briefly thought that was confirmation she had a secret twin and her mind started racing. “At this point, I’m making excuses for my whole life. This is why I’m co-dependent, because my twin was ripped away from me. I’ve always felt something missing, and this is why I have addiction issues.”

In reality, Tina Teigen didn't gasp because she was shocked that a massive family secret was coming out. Instead, Tina Teigen gasped because she instantly understood the hilarious reason behind Teigen's mistaken belief she had a twin.

Since Teigen has been a celebrity for many years now, she has gotten the chance to have some fascinating experiences. One example of that is Teigen once was asked to appear on the show Finding Your Roots.

As Teigen revealed in her Instagram video, her episode of Finding Your Roots didn't air, and they stopped filming partway through the process. However, by that point, the show's production staff had sent in her DNA sample for testing.

Because Finding Your Roots didn't want any of their guest's genetic tests to leak before each episode aired, they sent samples under fake names. Since Teigen's second sample was sent under her real name, 23andMe had no way of knowing the two samples were from the same person.

At the end of Teigen's Instagram video, she hilariously remarked. “My identical twin was myself. I was matching myself.”

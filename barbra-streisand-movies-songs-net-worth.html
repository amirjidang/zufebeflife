11 Barbra Streisand's Meteoric Rise To Stardom

Despite having a difficult early life, Barbra Streisand found solace in performing. Her powerful and unique voice set her apart from her peers, leading her to perform at various small clubs and bars. It was her role in the 1964 Broadway musical Funny Girl that catapulted her to fame.

Streisand's portrayal of Fanny Brice not only earned her a Tony Award but also showcased her incredible singing ability, captivating audiences around the world. Now she is known around the world for many different talents and reasons, including being one of, if not the most, demanding entertainers to work with or even interview for that matter.

10 Singing Sensation Barbra Streisand

Barbra Streisand's music career is a cornerstone of her financial success. With her distinctive voice and emotive delivery, she has released numerous albums that have achieved both critical acclaim and commercial success. Her debut album, The Barbra Streisand Album (1963), won two Grammy Awards, setting the stage for a series of successful releases. Hits like "People," "The Way We Were," and "Evergreen" solidified her status as a singing sensation.

9 Barbra Streisand's Films And Acting

Barbra Streisand's talents aren't confined to music; she has also made a significant impact in the film industry. Her acting debut in the 1968 film adaptation of Funny Girl earned her an Academy Award for Best Actress. This accomplishment made her the first woman to win an Oscar for both acting and singing. Streisand's acting career flourished with roles in movies like The Way We Were (1973) and Yentl (1983), which she also directed.

8 Barbra Streisand Behind The Camera

Barbra Streisand's talents extend beyond performing and acting; she's also proven her prowess behind the camera. Her directorial debut with Yentl showcased her as a multifaceted artist capable of handling various aspects of filmmaking.

The film's critical acclaim and box office success further added to her wealth. Streisand continued to direct and produce films, imprinting her unique style on each project. Her ability to navigate the complex world of Hollywood both in front of and behind the camera speaks volumes about her acumen and adaptability.

7 Barbra Streisand Fragrances

Barbra Streisand ventured into the world of perfumery by launching her line of fragrances. The "Barbra Streisand Fragrances" brand offered a range of scents that bore her personal touch.

Known for her refined taste, Streisand's involvement in creating unique fragrances added a sense of authenticity to her brand. The line allowed her fans to connect with her on a different level, embracing her style and essence through the products she endorsed.

6 The Barbra Streisand Foundation

While not a traditional business venture, Barbra Streisand's philanthropic efforts have had a significant impact. The Streisand Foundation, established in 1986, focuses on supporting a range of causes, including women's rights, environmental conservation, civil liberties, and education.

Through her foundation, Streisand has made substantial donations to organizations that align with her values and priorities. Her philanthropic endeavors reflect her commitment to creating positive change in society.

5 Barbra Streisand's Real Estate

Barbra Streisand's investments in real estate have been as impressive as her performances. One notable purchase was her Malibu estate, which she bought in 1977 for around $250,000.

Over the years, she expanded the property and added her personal touch to create a lavish retreat. In 2017, she sold the estate for a staggering $15 million, showcasing her keen eye for real estate opportunities.

4 Barbra Streisand's Interior Design And Architecture

Barbra Streisand's interest in interior design and architecture has led her to invest in properties that she can customize according to her aesthetic preferences. Her Malibu estate, for example, underwent extensive renovations and additions under her supervision.

This involvement in the design and architecture of her properties highlights her passion for creating spaces that reflect her style and personality.

2 Barbra Streisand's Media And Publishing

The book provides readers with a glimpse into her homes, design philosophies, and personal aesthetics. Through her writing, Streisand has engaged with her audience on a different level, sharing her passions and interests beyond her artistic performances.

The revelations from the Takoda Collins autopsy, carried out by the Montgomery County Coroner’s Office, were deeply unsettling and sent shockwaves through the community. 

On December 13, 2019, the world was forever changed as Takoda’s young life came to a tragic end at Dayton Children’s Hospital. 

Despite his tender age, Takoda’s journey was marred by a history of heartache – a tale of abuse, neglect, and profound pain. 

His passing was an emotional blow that brought to light the immense suffering he had endured, painting a heartbreaking picture of the challenges he faced.

Takoda Collins Mom: A complex web of custody battles

Takoda Collins’ life started amidst a tumultuous backdrop of custody disputes and parental conflicts.

He was born on December 18, 2008, to Al-Muhatah McLean and Robin Collins in Wisconsin, Takoda’s early years were marked by instability. 

In a distressing turn of events, Takoda’s mother, Robin Collins, admitted to hitting him, leading to the removal of custody from her. 

Subsequent struggles with drugs and probation violations further eroded her parental rights, resulting in McLean gaining full custody of Takoda.

Takoda Collins age

Takoda Collins, a young soul burdened by a life of hardship and adversity, faced a series of challenges that defied his tender age.

His time on this earth was died, leaving behind a story that resonates with the urgency of addressing the issues he endured.

Takoda Collins’ father 

Al-Muhatah McLean, Takoda’s father, emerged as a central and troubling figure in the heart-wrenching narrative that unfolded around Takoda Collins. 

McLean’s history is marked by a troubling pattern of violence and abuse that cast a dark and ominous shadow over his young son’s life. 

Disturbingly, the accusations of mistreatment and harm weren’t confined solely to Takoda’s case. 

Even those closest to McLean, such as his fiancée Amanda Hinze, fell victim to his violent tendencies. 

What’s truly astonishing is that despite these serious allegations and a clear history of harm, the legal system seemed to falter in holding McLean accountable for his actions. 

This failure of justice allowed McLean’s destructive behaviour to persist, inflicting irreparable damage on those unfortunate enough to cross his path.

Takoda Collins murder story

The harrowing details of Takoda Collins’ murder paint a devastating picture of cruelty and tragedy that unfolded within the very walls of his home. 

The events leading up to his untimely death are a painful account of neglect and abuse that defy comprehension and tug at the heartstrings of anyone who hears his story.

According to the accounts provided by prosecutors, the final hours of Takoda’s life were marked by unspeakable horror. 

His own father, Al-Muhatah McLean, subjected him to a series of unimaginable acts of violence and brutality that no child should ever endure. 

The description of these acts is deeply disturbing and highlights the depths of depravity that can exist within some individuals.

McLean’s actions included physically assaulting Takoda, resorting to punching and elbowing him with alarming force. 

Perhaps even more shocking is the revelation that McLean stood on Takoda’s back, exerting immense pressure and causing unbearable pain. 

But the torment didn’t stop there. McLean forced Takoda into what can only be described as a torturous “punishment pose,” making him bend over and remain in that painful position for extended periods.

Incredibly, even as McLean went about his day, Takoda was left in this excruciating position, subjected to continuous suffering. 

As if this weren’t enough, McLean proceeded to escalate the cruelty by hurling demeaning insults and even pouring hot sauce on Takoda’s body. 

The young boy was further subjected to physical abuse, being thrown around and forcibly grabbed by his ears.

The culmination of this horrific ordeal unfolded in the bathroom, where McLean dragged Takoda and submerged him in a bathtub, ultimately leading to his drowning. 

The Autopsy Report

Takoda Collins autopsy conducted on Takoda Collins uncovered distressing evidence of the horrors he experienced in his final moments. 

The Montgomery County Coroner’s Office determined that his cause of death resulted from a harrowing combination of blunt force trauma, compressive asphyxia, and water submersion in a bathtub. 

The examination highlighted fractures in his ribs and skull, indicative of repeated impacts, along with signs of compressive injuries around his neck. 

These findings starkly illuminated the torment inflicted upon him, painting a chilling picture of the suffering he endured.

The culprits and consequences

The people responsible for causing Takoda Collins’ suffering were finally held accountable for their actions. 

Three people named Al-Muhatah McLean, Amanda Hinze, and Jennifer Ebert were charged for their involvement in Takoda’s death. 

McLean admitted guilt for various serious offences, such as murder, rape, kidnapping, and putting a child in danger. 

Hinze and Ebert also accepted responsibility by pleading guilty to charges of involuntary manslaughter and endangering a child. 

While these convictions brought some sense of closure, they also emphasized how important it is to prevent such heartbreaking incidents from happening in the first place. 

The tragic story of Takoda Collins reminds everyone about the urgent need to be watchful, informed, and proactive in cases involving child abuse and neglect.

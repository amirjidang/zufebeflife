Lisa Rinna Supports Kate Hudson's Boob Job

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  173 words  |  Billy Koelling

Lisa Rinna — who is no stranger to plastic surgery herself — says she supports Kate Hudson's decision to get a boob job.

"I think everyone needs to do whatever they need to do to make themselves feel good," she told UsMagazine.com at Us Weekly's Hot Hollywood bash Thursday. "And it's nobody’s business!"

Hudson's surgery has caused a mini Internet controversy.

The Hills' Spencer Pratt — whose wife Heidi has a "size E or F" bra size after multiple surgeries — dissed: "How is it possible to get breast implants and still not have breasts"?

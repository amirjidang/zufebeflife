As an actress and activist, Alyssa Milano is an outspoken celebrity. Still, one of her double standards on the police infuriated many people. Even if Alyssa Milano has become one of the most well-known activists for her outspoken personality, her fame started from being the star of Who’s The Boss in her childhood.

As a talented child, Alyssa Milano stole the hearts of millions of people with her charismatic personality. In her adult life, Alyssa Milano continues to lose more of her fans due to her controversial actions and opinions shared on social media.

Alyssa Milano Isn’t Afraid To Speak Her Mind

When not acting in movies, Alyssa Milano still found a way to engage with her fans on social media by voicing her opinions on women’s rights, politics, policies, and many others. Many of her fans appreciate her involvement in important issues in society, but others don't.

Being active on social media, Alyssa Milano often discusses controversial issues related to violence, guns, and women’s rights.

Although she is vocal about her appreciation for President Joe Biden, she was arrested when protesting for voting rights outside the White House. Along with 24 other activists, Alyssa Milano demanded more attention to voting rights.

Her involvement in activism, especially in raising awareness about wronged policies, continued by comparing America’s gun ownership law with the one in Japan.

Starting another debate on social media about the multiple assessments needed in Japan to own a gun, Alyssa Milano expressed her annoyance with the easiness of doing the same thing in America.

No matter how many important social issues she raises with her fans, people still think Alyssa Milano should be canceled.

Fans Think Alyssa Has Double Standards About Police

Alyssa Milano’s transparency in voicing her opinions on social media came with some backlash when fans thought her values contradict each other. As an activist, she often judged the patriarchy, and she encouraged victims of sexual harassment and abuse to use #MeToo on Twitter and talk about their experiences.

Another popular topic she promotes is defunding the police by removing funds from their departments and reallocating them for other purposes for community support.

When she called a significant police response for what seemed to be a false alarm, people wanted to push her to be canceled quickly.

Alyssa Milano Infuriates Fans With Posts On Social Media

Many fans still follow her on social media due to her movies, while others appreciate Alyssa Milano for using her celebrity platform to raise awareness of certain societal issues. She infuriated many fans online when she questioned Tara Reade’s accusations against Biden, diminishing her credibility on the #MeToo movement.

“I think Milano is despicable for this,” wrote one fan.

Even if after this incident Alyssa Milano continued to promote her crucial involvement in the #MeToo movement, people were not so impressed anymore. If this incident was not enough to cancel her, another backlash came when Alyssa Milano got the idea of wearing a mask wrong during COVID-19.

She was slammed by many people for having holes in her mask and not being protective enough. Fans weren't happy because of these, but also because of her double standards on the #Metoo movement and on the police.

Alyssa Milano Caused Controversy From The Beginning Of Her Career

After she stole millions of hearts during her eight successful seasons on Who’s The Boss, Alyssa Milano’s charismatic personality brought her more gigs. This sitcom skyrocketed her fame as a star whose popularity continued to increase in the following years.

Shortly after this peak, Alyssa Milano found herself again in the spotlight as Phoebe in Charmed.

This role not only secured her fame for another eight successful seasons but also added big bucks to her bank account. Even though they seemed inseparable on screen, Alyssa Milano and the cast of Charmed aren't "besties" in real life.

In what started as a debate on politics on Twitter between Alyssa Milano and Rose McGowan, her co-star revealed specific negative experiences of working with Alyssa Milano on Charmed. It seems that she wasn't the easiest person to work with, according to Rose McGowan.

Rose McGowan Called Alyssa Milano A "Liar"

Since Charmed was watched by millions of people for over eight successful seasons, fans weren't happy when the co-stars of the series fought on Twitter over politics and their sincerity on different subjects. Ever since the show finished in 2006, Rose McGowan has accused her former co-star of lying about her real interests in politics and activism.

Although she managed to remain current by voicing her controversial opinions on social media and involving in different movements, Alyssa Milano's acting career is not the focus of the people who follow her now.

If Alyssa Milano isn’t canceled already, many people think she should be because of having contradicting values toward patriarchy, police, and women’s rights.

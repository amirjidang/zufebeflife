What January Jones Has Been Up To Since The End Of 'Mad Men', And What's Her Net Worth Today?

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1130 words  |  Billy Koelling

Highlights

  • January Jones, known for her role as Betty Draper in Mad Men, has continued her successful acting career and recently starred in the film 'God Is a Bullet.'
  • In addition to acting, Jones has pursued other interests, such as tap dancing and modeling. She has worked with numerous brands and appeared on the covers of various publications.
  • Jones is actively involved in humanitarian work, including volunteering at charity events and participating in causes such as the Neighborhood Protest for Equality. She has shown her commitment to giving back to the community.

The television show Mad Men is still being streamed today, despite its conclusion in 2015. AMC showed the period drama, which centered on an advertising agency in New York City during the 1960s, for a total of seven seasons. During that period, the show and its stars were honored with the Screen Actors Guild Award, the Golden Globe Award, and 16 Emmy Awards.

Even though the show has been definitively finished, and the finale featured a montage of the outcomes for the main characters, the actors who played those roles had only just begun their real lives. Since then, January Jones, cast in the role of Betty Draper, has been featured in several movies and shows, in addition to modeling and volunteering for charitable organizations.

Following the conclusion of the Mad Men television series, here is everything that January Jones has been doing since then.

Updated September 2023: January Jones has been acting for over 20 years. However, it was her role as Betty Draper in 'Mad Men' that really got her career off the ground.

Since then, Jones has experienced success on both the big and small screens, most recently in the film, 'God Is a Bullet.' All of this has helped Jones build her net worth of $10 million.

As a result of the WGA and SAG-AFTRA strikes, the projects that Jones was going to work on are in a state of pre-production. Once the strikes come to an end, Jones will hopefully be jumping right back in where she left off with her acting career and will be on-screen through 2024 and beyond.

8 January Jones Played A Part In A Post-Apocalyptic Sitcom

Jones starred as Melissa Shart, a courageous survivor who wears goofy boots, on the post-apocalyptic sitcom The Last Man on Earth.

The series is set in the late 2020s, after a global pandemic wiped out most of humanity. It revolves around Phil Miller (Will Forte), who appears to be the last human alive. Miller travels from state to state, painting signs proclaiming his survival, but he finds no one in his native Tucson, Arizona, despite his efforts to contact them.

7 January Jones Took Up Tap Dancing

Back in the year 2020, when the stay-at-home measures were implemented in Southern California, Jones, like many other people, started participating in various pastimes, Jones learned tap dancing.

Jones went on to attempt her hand at dancing after getting the motivation from watching Annie, and she even bought some tap shoes.

6 January Jones Partnered With Dolce & Gabbana

After being discovered as a model while still in high school, Jones launched her professional career.

Jones has become a model thanks to advertisements for companies like Abercrombie & Fitch, Versace, Clearasil, and Kerastase. She has also graced the covers of numerous publications during her career, including Allure, Rolling Stone, Town & Country, The Hollywood Reporter, Harper's Bazaar, Marie Claire, Tatler, Glamour, Red, Cosmopolitan, Grazia, Tulia, Tu Style, Maxim, and GQ.

In 2021, Jones posed in a million-dollar pink dress from Dolce & Gabbana for the brand's official Instagram page.

5 January Jones Worked With Gwyneth Paltrow

The actress made the casting announcement on Instagram two months before her official debut in The Politician, which premiered on Netflix on September 27, 2019. She starred in the series and got to work with Gwyneth Paltrow.

Jones played Lizbeth Sloan, a rich housewife who was bored with her life. To make her character that much more rich, it was revealed that Sloan was also Astrid's step-mother, not to mention a former escort.

4 January Jones Appeared On Netflix's 'Spinning Out'

Jones had a Netflix appearance in the series Spinning Out. Jones portrays Carol Baker, a former athlete who gave up on the Olympics after becoming pregnant at a young age. Twenty-one years later, she is completely consumed by her daughters' skating careers, Kat and Serena.

Although Spinning Out gained a dedicated following, the show was canceled because its viewership statistics were not high enough to convince Netflix to produce a second season.

3 January Jones Starred In A Film Alongside Jamie Foxx

After Spinning Out was canceled, it seems like Jones has disappeared from both the big and small screens. Yet in 2021, it was announced that Jones would be joining the cast of God is a Bullet alongside Jamie Foxx, Nikolaj Coster-Waldau, Maika Monroe, and Andrew Dice Clay.

2 January Jones Is Actively Involved In Humanitarian Tasks

The actress who does not seem to age joined various humanitarian events and causes, from swimming with sharks to participating in Neighborhood Protest for Equality with her son.

In 2021, Jones and Malin Akerman volunteered at Los Angeles Mission's annual Thanksgiving event, which is their most recent charity work. After spending the morning cooking for the celebration, she posed for pictures with a couple of her new friends from the center.

Volunteers were on hand to serve dish meals for the community's homeless residents for the yearly event, which had to be tweaked significantly owing to COVID-19 protocols.

1 January Jones Had A Mini 'Mad Men' Get Together

Even if a spinoff of the popular AMC series Mad Men never happened, fans at least got a brief reunion of the female leads in 2021.

Jones, who played Betty Draper, posted a photo on Instagram of her mini-reunion with Kiernan Shipka, who played Sally Draper, and Christina Hendricks, who portrayed secretary-turned-office-manager Joan Harris, who played Peggy.

Shipka also shared another photo from the reunion on her Instagram story, and it's evident that the actors had a great time catching up. Jones and Shipka re-created their Golden Globes outfits from a decade ago and posted the photos to social media earlier this year. They also reunited for a series of glam images for the fashion company Rodarte in 2019.

Reese Witherspoon's Current Relationship Status In 2024 Surprised Fans

Highlights

  • Reese Witherspoon is taking her time with dating, not rushing into anything after her recent divorce.
  • The actress has been set up on a few dates with friends of friends, keeping things slow and steady.
  • False rumors have surfaced about Witherspoon being linked to Kevin Costner and Tom Brady, which her rep has denied.

Dating in the world of the rich and famous is drastically different from what us regular folks take part in. After all, everything these people do is blasted to millions of fans, so they tend to be far more secretive and selective about things. Sure, some are more than happy to have all aspects of their lives covered ad nauseum, but most tend to do the opposite.

Reese Witherspoon's recent divorce has been a major talking point for celebrity media, but the actress is not approaching dating in the way that some were expecting.

Let's take a look at how Reese Witherspoon is approaching her dating life!

Reese Witherspoon Is Taking Her Time With Dating, Sources Revealed

Newly single Reese Witherspoon is back in the dating scene, but instead of setting out to find love quickly, the actress is deciding to take her time.

According to a source speaking with US Weekly, "Reese hasn’t been out on too many dates, but she’s been set up with a few friends of friends."

The site also noted that the source revealed that Witherspoon is "taking things very slowly."

US Weekly also spoke to a second source, who noted that Witherspoon was never anticipating being back in the dating pool after she married Jim Toth some time ago.

That source specifically used the adjective "surreal" to describe Reese dating again after 12 years.

"In an ideal world, she’d love to meet someone special in 2024 and eventually settle down again," the source said.

However, that source noted "for now, there’s no pressure" for Witherspoon to find love sooner rather than later.

Back in November, rumors were swirling that Witherspoon was linked to actor, Kevin Costner, but this proved to be false. False rumors also surfaced that Witherspoon was linked to former NFL star, Tom Brady.

"The rumors are completely false," a rep told US Weekly.

As we previously mentioned, Witherspoon was previously married to Jim Toth, but things fell apart.

Witherspoon Was Previously Married To Jim Toth For 12 Years

The pair met at a party, when Jim came to her rescue.

"It happened out of the blue. This really drunk guy was hitting on me, making such an idiot of himself, yelling at me. He was, like, [slurring, scowling, pointing finger in her face] 'You don't know me.' And I was like, 'Yeah, I know. I don't know you!'" Witherspoon revealed to Elle.

"Jim came over and said, 'Please excuse my friend. He's just broken up with someone.' Jim was a really good friend, pulling him out of that situation. That's just kind of who he is, a really good person," she added.

According to PEOPLE, things really got rolling between the former partners back in 2010.

"Witherspoon and Toth first sparked dating rumors in February 2010 after they were spotted having dinner together in Santa Monica. By that December, they were engaged," the site reports.

The pair were married the following year, and while they seemed to have a good thing going, things soured, and a few days before their anniversary, it was announced that their union was over.

In their announcement, the couple said, "We have some personal news to share … It is with a great deal of care and consideration that we have made the difficult decision to divorce. We have enjoyed so many wonderful years together and are moving forward with deep love, kindness and mutual respect for everything we have created together"

Prior to her romance with Toth, Witherspoon spent years with fellow actor, Ryan Phillippe.

Prior To This, She Was Married To Ryan Phillippe, And Had Two Children With Him

According to PEOPLE, "After starring in the hit film Cruel Intentions together, Witherspoon and Phillippe quickly became a beloved duo. The young couple ended up getting married and went on to have two kids, their daughter Ava and son Deacon."

These two young stars were the It Couple for years, but eventually, things went south, and they split up.

In a statement, the couple's rep said, "We are saddened to announce that Reese and Ryan have decided to formally separate. They remain committed to their family and we ask that you please respect their privacy and the safety of their children at this time."

Since then, they have co-parented their children.

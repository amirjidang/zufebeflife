Did He Cheat On Miley? Cody Simpson's Side Of The Story

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  218 words  |  Martina Birk

Miley Cyrus and Cody Simpson are supposedly an item, but after rumors were spread that Simpson was seen out with other women in NYC, there has been doubt about his fidelity.

A Flock Of Females

According to E! News, Simpson was partying at Little Sister in NYC and dancing behind the DJ booth with a bunch of females, when one allegedly kissed him. When he and the folks he was with left to go someplace else, these gals tagged along. Simpson denies any of it, with his agent saying on his behalf, “There is absolutely no truth to this story.”

Playboy Playmate

Perhaps what the agent says is true, but when Simpson was seen trotting around Manhattan with Playboy Playmate Jordy Murray over the weekend, more rumors swirled. Before folks go into a frenzy, note that Murray is the girlfriend of actor Ryan McCarthy, a friend of Simpson’s.

Any Truth To It All?

Simpson’s sister claims he’s with Cyrus, and that’s that. As a celeb, it’s no shock that fans would flock around the singer, and it’s hard to believe he’d shoo them away. As for that kiss? Simpson’s agent added, “Looks can be deceiving.” Guess we’ll have to trust that Simpson’s being faithful to Cyrus. If she can deal with the gossip, then good for her.

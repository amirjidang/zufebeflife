Glee first hit our screens in 2009, and went on to become one of the most popular and successful TV shows at the time. During its six-year rein, fans enjoyed watching a 'group of misfits' transform to become a successful choir group, all while dealing with a variety of issues such as family issues, teen relationships, sexuality, race and teamwork. The show is highly rated among fans and critics, highlighting both its current and former success as a series.

Thanks to the shows raging success, many of the cast members managed to build solid net worths. Some of the richest cast members include Jane Lynch, who is worth $16 million, Harry Shum Jr, Lea Michele (whose first audition actually didn't go too smoothly), Matthew Morrison, and Chris Colfer, all sporting net worths above $8 million US dollars.

Blake Jenner, who was best known for playing the role of Ryder Lynn, has a total net worth of $1.5 million dollars. However, he has been at the center of some controversies over the past years.

What Movies Has Blake Jenner Been In?

While best known for his role in Glee, the young actor has also starred in several other movies. In his early teenage years, he starred in many TV commercials before moving up the ladder. His first appearance was in the 2010 short Fresh 2 Death. He later went on to appear in the TV series Melissa & Joey and the film Cousin Sarah in 2011.

Just a year later, he managed to land a life-changing role as Ryder Lynn in the Fox musical comedy-drama show called Glee. It was this role that would go on to open many more doors for the LA-based actor.

Since then, Jenner has gone on to star in The Edge of Seventeen (2016), Everybody Wants Some!! Everybody Wants Some!! (2016), American Animals (2018), Billy Boy (2017), and the TV series What/If.

Why Was Glee Star Blake Jenner Arrested?

More recently, the 29-year-old actor has been getting himself in hot water. So, what exactly has Blake Jenner been arrested for?

Earlier last month, Blake Jenner was arrested for driving under the influence in California and obtained DUI charges. On July 9th, he was pulled over after officers spotted him failing to come to a stop at a red light.

He Also Publicly Addressed Domestic Violence Allegations In 2020

However, his recent driving incident isn't the only controversy the young actor has been caught up in. Just two years ago in 2020, Jenner took to his Instagram to post a heartfelt message, addressing domestic violence allegations concerning his ex-wife Melissa Benoist.

The heartfelt explanation spanned a total of six pages, and it seemed as though Jenner clearly had a lot to get off his chest in regard to the situation. He began by explaining that he has been reflecting on how to handle the situation and that he now felt that it was an issue that needed to be addressed.

"Throughout that time, I reflected on a period in my life that I had previously kept in the darkness out of shame and fear, but I know this is something that needs to be addressed, not just publicly, but also privately with the individual directly affected and with myself."

He then proceeds to open up about his former relationship with his wife, explaining elements of their relationship for the public. He actually dives into quite some detail.

"When I was 20 years old, I met a woman and we fell in love. I didn't realize the magnitude of it back then, but in retrospect, as great as the love that was shared between us, the shared brokenness that stemmed from our childhoods proved to be greater."

The post then dives into further depth and detail. His ex-wife also opened up about the situation, detailing on a video on Instagram how she thought she'd 'never be sharing a story like this'.

She has since gone on to share her side of the story in hopes of helping other people in the same situation as her. Since then, neither side seems to of spoken publicly about the issue again.

Everything We Know About Mtley Cre Star Nikki Sixx's Son, Gunner Nicholas

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1153 words  |  Billy Koelling

Mötley Crüe bassist Nikki Sixx is a talented musician and has had a long and successful career, but his personal life is also of great interest. He may have millions of fans around the world, but he is also a father, a role he seems to take very seriously. Nikki is a dad to five children, only two of which are sons, and his oldest is Gunner Nicholas Sixx.

Gunnar was born on Jan. 25, 1991, and is a fascinating and talented individual carving out his own path (although not that much is truly known about him). But he does have some similarities with his famous dad, including a shared love of music. So, who is Gunnar Sixx, the son of one of the world's most legendary rockers, and what do you need to know about him? We are going to find out.

8 Gunnar Sixx Is One Of Nikki Sixx's Five Children

Nikki Sixx may be a father of five, but he shares his first three children with his ex-wife, Brandi Brandt. In addition to his son, Gunner Nicholas Sixx, who is the oldest, the couple also has a second son, Decker, and a daughter, Storm (this means Gunnar has two full siblings and two step-siblings). Nikki also has an adult daughter, Frankie, with Donna D'Errico (best known for her role in Baywatch and her modeling with Playboy), and a toddler, Ruby, who the rockstar shares with his current wife, Courtney Sixx.

7 Gunnar Sixx's Mother Is Brandi Brandt

Brandi Brandt is a model and former Playboy Playmate. She was married to Nikki Sixx from 1989 to 1997 and the former couple shares three children, the oldest being Gunnar. In 2014, Brandt made headlines for her legal issues as she found herself embroiled in a substance smuggling scandal that saw her face up to six years in prison. According to CBC, she had pleaded guilty "to a charge of conspiring to import drugs."

Her defense attorney Phillip Boulten had asked the court for leniency regarding the matter, bringing up Brandt's "pretty mixed-up childhood."

6 Gunnar Sixx Is A Musician Like His Father

Gunnar Sixx is also a musician and this is something that his famous father is very proud of. The band in question is Figs Vision, an alternative/indie genre, and features Gunnar Sixx among its members.

According to LoudWire, Nikki shared his praise for the band on social media, writing, "I love music. As you know I am always looking for new music not only for my ears but also for other people as well. I have been very open about creativity and supporting music over the years in my books and blogs. As a father, you hope to pass this on to your kids. So with that being said my oldest son Gunner and his songwriting partner Jordan Spoliansky been collaborating on new music together, and I want to pass it on to you. (sic)"

He also shared their social media page and urged fans to check them out.

5 Gunnar Sixx Is An Incredibly Creative Individual

On his social media, the first two descriptions are "music/visual art," and this is a place where you can experience both. In addition to being a passionate musician, Gunnar Sixx has also shared photos of artwork, which we can only assume is his because you can message him to ask for pricing.

He accompanies the photos with a caption about the materials used to create each piece, for example, acrylic and charcoal on canvas. He also shares a glimpse into the inspiration for each design. For this particular image, he wrote, "Hey yeah it's my birthday and I just finished this painting. I am surrounded by the sweetest creatures on the planet and I wake up every day excited to be where I am in my life. I hope this painting captures that appreciation and love."

4 Gunnar Sixx Gave Nikki Sixx The Best Job Of His Life

While we cannot pretend to know the ins and outs of Nikki Sixx's relationship with his son, Gunnar, what we do know is what he has said publicly. That is how much fatherhood means to him. "I didn't have a father, and I always loved being a father," he said, according to USA Today. "It's an itch I can't scratch. My kids are older, and I have a great relationship with them."

3 Nikki Sixx's Children Have Taught Him So Much

Nikki Sixx has been vocal about the challenges in his childhood. His father left when he was three and his mother left when he was six. In an interview, he explained how she rang up his grandparents to come and get him. When he has discussed his own family, things are much happier. According to The Guardian, in a 2015 interview, before the birth of his fifth child (daughter, Ruby) he had this to say about his kids: "I have four amazing children, and it's been the greatest thing that’s ever happened in my life. They teach me every day. I try to show them what I've learned."

2 Gunnar Sixx Is A Dog Dad

If you take a glance at his social media, which Gunnar Sixx is active on and appears to have a decent following, you will see that he is a dog dad (Nikki Sixx also loves dogs and mentioned his beloved pooch, Leica, in an interview with The Guardian).

The photos he posts of his pet are adorable and really capture the love they share. Some people will argue that you can find out a lot about someone by seeing how they treat animals. There is even a quote believed to be attributed to The Beatles rock star Paul McCartney that states "You can judge a man's true character by the way he treats his fellow animals."

1 Gunnar Sixx Will Always Have The Support Of His Dad

What Gunnar Sixx has or has not done with his life is his business, and we are not here to dig into it. But what we do know is that his father, Nikki Sixx, tried to impart wisdom to him and his siblings with regard to taking drugs. Nikki Sixx's wild rocker days are well-documented, so when he addressed the topic it was challenging for him to do with a "straight face."

No matter what happens in my kids' lives, I'll be there for them. My family means everything to me and I will do anything for them."

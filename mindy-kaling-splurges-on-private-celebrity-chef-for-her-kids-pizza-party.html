Mindy Kaling Splurges On Private Celebrity Chef For Her Kids Pizza Party

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Tandra Barner

With a reported net worth of $35 million, Mindy Kaling is making sure to give her children the best of everything – including a private chef for an ultra-luxurious pizza party.

The Office alum enlisted the help of private celebrity chef, Brooke Baevsky, famously known as Chef Bae on social media.

In a peek behind the scenes, Baevsky, 27, took to Instagram on Wednesday, August 23rd, to share her culinary journey in preparing the delectable pies. "Join me as I take on the role of a private chef for a pizza bash with @mindykaling," she playfully captioned the video.

The footage showcased both Baevsky and the 44-year-old Kaling sampling slices of pizza, their nods of approval giving a clear thumbs-up to the mouthwatering creations. And Kaling herself joined in the fun, giving an enthusiastic thumbs-up to the camera.

Later in the video, the chef got assistance from Mindy’s two children, Katherine, 5, and Spencer, 2. “I start off by making the homemade pizza dough and the cutie kiddies help me and they do such a good job,” she narrate to the video, as she helps the kids stir the dough.

While Mindy’s children undoubtedly had fun during the pizza party, it may have cost the actress pretty penny. Estimates suggest that private chefs cost an average of $30 to $40, or $100 for a single dinner. It likely costs more if they’re hired to give a one-on-one tutorial, like Baevsky did for the actress and her kids.

This isn’t the only pricey activity Mindy recently completed with her kid. Earlier this month, she was spotted taking her daughter to one of Taylor Swift’s Eras tour stops in Los Angeles.

“Thank you so much @sofi and @taylorswift for the best first concert EVER for my daughter, Kit!” Mindy wrote via Instagram to commemorate the occasion. “She swapped friendship bracelets all night and got to hear her favorite Taylor song live, ‘Cruel Summer!’ It was such a magical night.”

Not only are Taylor Swift Eras tickets hard to obtain, but they’re also re-selling for ridiculous prices. People have reported spending thousands on tickets that original cost hundreds, and even worse, many people have been scammed by people selling fake tickets.

This speaks to the success of Taylor’s tour, which is on track to gross over $1 billion, making it one of the most successful tours in history.

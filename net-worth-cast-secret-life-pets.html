The Secret Life of Pets movies are hilarious and heartwarming films about the life pets lead when their owners aren't home. The first film broke the bank when it debuted so it isn't surprising that Hollywood jumped on the opportunity to make a sequel.

The films have attracted some top-notch talent like Kevin Hart, Harrison Ford, Dana Carvey, Ellie Kemper, and more. It's likely there will be more projects within this franchise in the future. We're looking at how wealthy the cast of these movies truly is in real-life.

Eric Stonestreet - $23 million

Eric Stonestreet is best-known for his work on Modern Family where he's played Cameron Tucker for 11 seasons now. The bulk of his $23 million net worth is definitely from his Modern Family paychecks. He plays Duke in both The Secret Life of Pets movies.

Related: The Entire Modern Family Cast Net Worth

Outside of those projects, Stonestreet is best-known for his recurring role on C.S.I., and the movies Identity Theft, The Loft, and Bad Teacher. He also appeared in an early episode of American Horror Story. Currently, Stonestreet is focusing on wrapping up his character on Modern Family as the show is currently in its final season.

Kevin Hart - $128 million

Kevin Hart is easily the biggest name in the franchise. He voices the nefarious bunny, Snowball. He has an astoundingly high net worth of $128 million thanks to his work in comedy. His stand-up specials and tours always perform really well and have helped make him a household name.

Related: Knives Out: Net Worth Of The Main Cast, Ranked

The comedian is known for his performances in several different films like Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, Night School, Get Hard, Central Intelligence, and much more. He'll next appear in the Jumanji sequel coming out this month.

Jenny Slate - $5 million

In The Secret Life of Pets, Jenny Slate voices the character Gidget. Her overall net worth is about $5 million. Jenny Slate's breakout role came when she was cast on Saturday Night Live. Since then, she has gone on to write her own book and even create her own stand-up comedy special.

Slate also played a recurring role on the beloved sitcom Parks and Recreation. She has also appeared on shows like Brooklyn Nine-Nine and voiced characters on Big Mouth, Bob's Burgers, and more. She'll next appear in the film On the Rocks directed by Sofia Coppola.

Lake Bell - $3 million

Lake Bell voiced the cat Chloe in both Secret Life of Pets films. She has a relatively low net worth of about $3 million overall. While Bell has been in the acting industry for many years, she tends to play more low-key roles and act in independent projects.

Related: 10 Richest Late Night Talk Show Hosts, Ranked By Net Worth

Right now she is currently starring in the sitcom Bless this Mess alongside Dax Shepard. In the past, she has voiced characters in shows like BoJack Horseman and Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse. Her most notable television role was in the show Boston Legal.

Tiffany Haddish - $2 million

Tiffany Haddish first broke out big-time when she starred in Girls Trip alongside Jada Pinkett Smith, Regina Hall, and Queen Latifah. Since then, she has been nabbing projects left and right.

Haddish has been vocal about her financial troubles in the past so it's nice to see she has a net worth of several million dollars now. She has also been vocal about her friendship with comedian Kevin Hart. Haddish gets to work with Hart again in The Secret Life of Pets 2 where she voices Daisy.

Ellie Kemper - $4 million

The bubbly actress Ellie Kemper voices the character of Katie in both Secret Life of Pets films. Outside of the animated world, she is best known for her work on The Office and Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt.

The actress has an overall net worth of $4 million, which actually seems a little low due to the popularity of the many series she's worked on in the past. Next year she'll star in the comedy The Stand-In alongside actress Drew Barrymore. She also served as a guest judge on America's Got Talent this past season.

Patton Oswalt - $14 million

Patton Oswalt was a new addition to The Secret Life of Pets 2 after the controversy surrounding Louis C.K. occurred. Oswalt took over C.K.'s previous voice acting role as Max. The comedian is extremely well-known and has been around in the industry for a long time.

Related: Every Cast Member of Futurama's Net Worth

His net worth of $14 million makes sense given how many projects he's worked on over the years. Some of his most notable projects include Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., Young Adult, Happy!, and his work as the narrator on the ABC sitcom The Goldbergs.

Nick Kroll - $10 million

Nick Kroll is an accomplished comedian in Hollywood probably best known for his impressive resume of voice-acting roles. Outside of voicing Sergei in The Secret Life of Pets 2, Kroll also had his own show on Comedy Central called the Kroll Show and he voiced characters in The Addams Family, Sausage Party, Sing, Bob's Burgers, and more.

Currently, he serves as the creator, writer, executive producer, and voice of several characters on Netflix's Big Mouth. Overall, he has a net worth of about $10 million.

Harrison Ford - $230 million

Obviously, Harrison Ford is the biggest name on this list with an astonishingly high net worth of $230 million. Considering his legacy in the Star Wars franchise, that is not at all surprising.

Related: Ranking All The Movies In The Star Wars Franchise By How Much They Grossed

Ford joined the cast of The Secret Life of Pets in the sequel, he played a dog named Rooster. He'll work with animals once again in the upcoming film, Call of the Wild. Ford is also known for his work in Blade Runner, The Fugitive, Indiana Jones, and much more. He has been nominated for many awards and starred in several award-winning films.

Dana Carvey - $20 million

Dana Carvey is definitely best known for his work on Saturday Night Live. He won a Primetime Emmy Award while working on the show and was nominated for several more.

If you're a fan of Carvey's you've probably seen his iconic films, Wayne's World and Wayne's World 2. He faded from the limelight for a few years but he's slowly been making a return as of late. He played the character Pops in both Secret Life of Pets films.

The Kardashian-Jenner family is known to love the finer things in life and showing off their luxuries on social media. They haven’t been shy to flaunt their incredible car collection. In fact, many of the family members have gifted each other cars on special occasions.

The family likely has hundreds of millions of dollars in cars between all of them. One report said that Kylie’s car collection is likely worth between “$50 million to $70 million.”

Below, we take a look at some of the most famous vehicles that belong to the Kardashian-Jenner clan.

Rob’s $400,000 Rolls Royce SUV

Nowadays, Rob Kardashian is mostly out of the spotlight. The paparazzi even struggle to keep up with him nowadays. But they did snap photos of him in a stunning Rolls Royce SUV in 2020, which is reportedly worth anything between $350,000 to 400,000.

Kim’s $400,000 Maybach Minivan

One of Kim’s most recent purchases is a Maybach minivan, which she reportedly spent $400,000 on in March 2022. She got it customized to her taste, with features like seat heaters and coolers, folding tables, multi-color roof lighting, and reclining chairs.

Kim’s purchase came months after Kanye bought his own minivan for $400,000, though he chose a Mercedes model. The rapper also got custom features, including studio-worthy speakers, multiple large TVs, tray tables, and swivel sweats.

It appears that Kim and Kanye’s four children are going to ride to school in style.

Kendall’s $500,000 SF90 Stradale

Kendall Jenner witnessed a fatal car crash in 2021 alongside her boyfriend Devin Booker. She was driving her SF90 Stradale when a truck pulling a tractor in front of her lost control and went over the side of a bridge.

She reportedly still owns the Stradale to this day, which is valued at $507,000.

Kim’s $600,000 Lamborghini Urus Mansory WideBody

According to Car HP, the 2021 Lamborghini Urus Mansory WideBody is the most expensive car in Kim’s collection, which starts at $600,000. She may have paid more for customizations.

Kendall’s $700,000 Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren

Kendall owns one of the most desired sports cars in history, a Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren. The supermodel likely spent at least $740,000 on the supercar.

Kylie Has Likely Spent Millions On Rolls Royces

Kylie has shown off that she has several Rolls Royces parked in her driveway. It’s unconfirmed how much the makeup mogul spent on her rides, but Royces are known to be expensive.

The vehicles start at $460,000, so with customizations and wanting the best model, Kylie may have easily spent over a million on one of her Royces. One of her Royces, for example, is a custom pink model with “Stormi’s Mom” engraved in the paneling.

Kendall’s $1.74 Million Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport Vitesse

Kendall has been spotted driving this luxurious Bugatti on a few occasions, which is valued at $1.74 million and is reportedly the most expensive car in her collection.

Kylie’s $2.2 Million LaFerrari Aperta

Kylie may be the biggest car lover in her family, given that she’s spent millions on single models. She reportedly bought a LaFerrari Aperta for $2.2 million, though the supercar has been sold for upwards of $5 million before.

Kylie’s $3 Million Bugatti Chiron

Kylie’s white Bugatti Chiron has been seen on her social media before, so at least she’s getting her money’s worth. The reality star reportedly dropped $3 million the luxury ride.

Khloe’s Million Dollar Car Collection

Though her younger sister may own a car worth $2 million alone, Khloe doesn’t have as expensive rides to her name. But she certainly doesn’t have a lacking car collection, either.

According to auto experts speaking to The List, Khloe’s collection has likely set her back $2.2 million, as she cars like Ferraris, Rolls Royces, and Bentleys in her garage.

"She certainly has a love for Rolls-Royce, owning several models from the luxury brand," Lawrence Allan, Motoring News Editor at Desperate Seller, told The List. "Like her sister Kylie, Khloé has a Cullinan,” which is valued at $300,000, he continued.

Khloe has even received criticism for having a parking garage for her 4-year-old daughter's play cars and scooters.

Kourtney Has Also Spent A Million On Cars

Out of all the Kardashian-Jenner sisters, Kourtney has proved to have simpler taste and she doesn’t have the same reputation for buying luxury goods. However, that hasn’t stopped her from spending nearly a million on cars.

The List estimates the mom-of-three has spent at least $1.01 million on cars, which includes a Rolls Royce Dawn valued at $300,000.

Kris Gifted Her Children Cars For Christmas

Kris Jenner doesn’t have the most expensive cars in her family, but she has a collection that’s likely worth millions. Some of the models she reportedly owns include a $237,000 Ferrari 488, a $300,000 Bentley Continental, and a $322,000 Rolls Royce Ghost.

What’s more, Kris has a history of gifting cars. In December 2021, she surprised her adult children with Moke electric vehicles as Christmas presents. She reportedly spent over $150,000 on the cars.

“Oh my god look at our Christmas presents from my mom, for all of her kids. How cool!” Kim said in a video posted to Instagram, which showed off the six cars, each in their own colors.

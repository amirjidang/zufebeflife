90 Day Fiance Couple Anny And Robert Expecting Baby No. 3 After Infant Sons Death

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  595 words  |  Patria Henriques

Highlights

  • Anny and Robert are expecting their third child together after tragically losing their infant son in 2022.
  • The couple subtly announced the pregnancy on Instagram, sparking excitement among fans of 90 Day Fiance.
  • Despite facing challenges, Anny and Robert's genuine affection for each other has endeared them to audiences.

There’s going to be another baby in the 90 Day Fiance family. Nearly two years after the loss of their infant son, Anny Francisco and Robert Springs revealed they’re expecting their third child together.

Robert recently took to his Instagram Stories on June 15th to post a photo featuring himself and his wife enjoying a beach outing. In the image, both Robert and Anny were dressed in pink, with Robert tenderly leaning down to kiss Anny's stomach.

Anny And Robert Subtly Announced Her Pregnancy

Despite not including a caption with the image, Robert acknowledged the current pregnancy speculation indirectly. He expressed gratitude towards the fan page 90 Day FiancéUpdates for their congratulations in a separate social media post, subtly confirming the ongoing excitement surrounding their growing family.

This public display on Instagram sparked widespread interest and well-wishes from fans, eagerly anticipating further updates from the couple as they embark on this new chapter together.

Anny and Robert captured the hearts of many 90 Day Fiance viewers with their unique love story. The couple's journey began when they met online and quickly developed a strong connection. Robert, who hails from Winter Park, Florida, decided to take a leap of faith and visited Anny in her home country of the Dominican Republic. After spending just eight hours together, he proposed to her, setting the stage for their appearance on the show's seventh season.

Robert And Anny Lost Their Infant Son In 2022

Their relationship was marked by numerous challenges, including cultural differences and financial struggles, which were prominently featured on the show. Despite these obstacles, Anny and Robert's genuine affection for each other shone through, endearing them to the audience.

One of the major milestones in their relationship was their wedding, which took place in September 2019.

Anny and Robert's fame continued to rise as they expanded their family. In April 2020, they welcomed their first child together, a daughter named Brenda Aaliyah. The couple faced another significant moment in September 2021, when they announced they were expecting their second child.

Tragically, their son Adriel passed away in April 2022, shortly after birth, due to undisclosed reasons, a heartbreaking event that brought an outpouring of support from the 90 Day Fiancé community.

“Today is one of the worst days of my life, my son Adriel Springs died,” Anny wrote in Spanish via Instagram in April 2022 when announcing their son’s loss. “I am devastated and [so is] my family … life is so hard and difficult. I never thought I would lose my son. He was a fighter … his heart couldn’t take it … my heart is broken it’s a big pain … so difficult.”

The couple faced a similar wave of support ad congratulations with the announcement that they’re expecting again, and fans of the franchise can’t wait to see what’s next for their family.

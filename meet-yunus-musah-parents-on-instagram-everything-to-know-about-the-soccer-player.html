Yunus Dimoara Musah was born on November 29, 2002, is an American professional soccer player who now represents Valencia in La Liga and the US national team.

He was born in the United States to Ghanaian parents and spent most of his childhood in England.

In October 2020, he got called to the under-19 squad.

On September 8, 2019, Musah’s penalty won England’s under-18s a draw against Brazil’s under-17s, and on October 16, 2019, he scored a crucial goal in England’s 3–2 victory against Austria’s under-18s.

Musah represented England at the youth level more than 30 times in total.
